# Remove Negative Thinking
**Author:** Helga Klopcic & KC Harry

## What This Book Is About
Remove Negative Thinking is a concise guide to harnessing mindfulness and positive thinking to overcome negative thought patterns. Part of The #GirlBizMind Series, it provides practical techniques for instantly shifting your mindset, breaking free from self-defeating mental habits, and cultivating a more empowered, positive outlook on life and business.

## Key Topics
- Identifying and disrupting negative thought patterns
- Mindfulness techniques for mental clarity
- The power of positive thinking in daily life and business
- Practical exercises for mindset transformation
- Building resilience against negativity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a quick, actionable guide to break free from negative thinking and reset your mental approach.
