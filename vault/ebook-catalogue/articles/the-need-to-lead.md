# The Need to Lead
**Author:** Dave Berke

## What This Book Is About
Part of the Extreme Ownership series, former Marine fighter pilot and Echelon Front instructor Dave Berke explores the fundamental human need for leadership at every level of an organization. The book provides battlefield-tested principles and business applications for stepping up, taking ownership, and leading effectively in any situation.

## Key Topics
- The universal need for leadership at all levels
- Extreme Ownership principles applied to business
- Decision-making under pressure
- Building trust and accountability in teams
- Military leadership lessons for civilian life

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want practical, no-nonsense leadership principles from the Extreme Ownership tradition.
