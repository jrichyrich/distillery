# Joseph Smith: Rough Stone Rolling
**Author:** Richard Lyman Bushman

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive, scholarly biography of Joseph Smith by Columbia historian Richard Bushman. Drawing on extensive primary sources including the Joseph Smith Papers, Bushman presents a nuanced portrait of the Mormon prophet that takes both his religious claims and historical context seriously, tracing his life from humble New England origins through the founding of a global religious movement.

## Key Topics
- Joseph Smith's family background and early visions
- The coming forth of the Book of Mormon
- Building the early Latter-day Saint church
- Political, social, and theological controversies
- Smith's legacy in American religious history

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want the definitive scholarly biography of Joseph Smith that engages honestly with both faith and history.
