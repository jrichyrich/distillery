# Playing to Win: How Strategy Really Works
**Author:** A.G. Lafley & Roger L. Martin

## What This Book Is About
Playing to Win distills the strategic framework that A.G. Lafley used as CEO of Procter & Gamble to double the company's value. Lafley and strategy advisor Roger Martin present a five-step strategic choice cascade -- aspiration, where to play, how to win, core capabilities, and management systems -- that applies to organizations of any size. The book is filled with real-world P&G examples showing how disciplined strategic choices drive competitive advantage.

## Key Topics
- The five-step strategic choice cascade
- Defining where to play and how to win
- Building and leveraging core capabilities
- Aligning management systems to strategy
- Making strategy a disciplined practice rather than a planning exercise
- Real-world case studies from Procter & Gamble brands

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a clear, practical framework for making strategic choices that create sustainable competitive advantage.
