# Shoulder Pain? The Solution & Prevention
**Author:** John M. Kirsch, M.D.

## What This Book Is About
Shoulder Pain? The Solution & Prevention presents Dr. John Kirsch's evidence-based approach to treating and preventing shoulder pain through simple hanging exercises. An orthopedic surgeon, Dr. Kirsch argues that many shoulder problems -- including impingement, rotator cuff issues, and frozen shoulder -- can be addressed without surgery by restoring the natural anatomy of the shoulder through regular brachiating (hanging) movements. Now in its fourth edition, revised and expanded.

## Key Topics
- The anatomy and biomechanics of the shoulder
- How hanging exercises restore shoulder function
- Non-surgical treatment for common shoulder conditions
- Rotator cuff injury prevention and rehabilitation
- Evidence and imaging supporting the hanging approach
- Exercise protocols for shoulder health

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are dealing with shoulder pain and want to explore a simple, non-surgical approach before considering more invasive treatments.
