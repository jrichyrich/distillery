# Managing Risk and Information Security
**Author:** Malcolm Harkins

## What This Book Is About
A strategic guide to information security that reframes the security function from a cost center and obstacle into a business enabler. Harkins, drawing on his experience as Intel's CISO, presents a "Protect to Enable" philosophy that aligns security programs with business objectives while managing risk effectively.

## Key Topics
- The "Protect to Enable" security philosophy
- Aligning security with business strategy
- Risk management frameworks for information security
- Building a security program that enables innovation
- The evolving role of the CISO

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to rethink your security program as a business enabler rather than a roadblock.
