# Courage Is Calling
**Author:** Ryan Holiday

## What This Book Is About
The first book in Ryan Holiday's Stoic Virtues series, focusing on courage. Holiday draws on stories of historical figures who exemplified bravery -- from ancient philosophers to modern leaders -- to explore what courage means, how fear holds us back, and how we can cultivate the bravery needed to live a meaningful life.

## Key Topics
- Stoic philosophy and the virtue of courage
- Overcoming fear and cowardice
- Historical examples of bravery
- The call to action and heroic courage
- Distinguishing true courage from recklessness
- Daily practices for building courage

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need inspiration to face a difficult decision or situation with courage, or when you want a philosophical grounding in why bravery matters.
