# Stealing Fire
**Author:** Steven Kotler & Jamie Wheal

## What This Book Is About
Stealing Fire explores how Silicon Valley executives, Navy SEALs, maverick scientists, and other high-performers are using altered states of consciousness -- or "ecstasis" -- to dramatically accelerate performance, creativity, and collaboration. Kotler and Wheal examine four forces that can trigger these non-ordinary states: psychology, neurobiology, pharmacology, and technology. The book maps a revolution in human performance that is happening largely beneath public awareness.

## Key Topics
- The science of flow states and altered consciousness
- How elite performers access peak states
- The four forces of ecstasis: psychology, neurobiology, pharmacology, technology
- Applications in military, business, and creative domains
- The risks and ethics of consciousness hacking
- The emerging "altered states economy"

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand how cutting-edge performers are using the science of altered states to unlock extraordinary levels of creativity and productivity.
