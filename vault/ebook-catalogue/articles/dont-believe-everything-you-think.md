# Don't Believe Everything You Think
**Author:** Joseph Nguyen

## What This Book Is About
A concise exploration of how our own thinking creates suffering, and how recognizing this pattern is the key to inner peace. Nguyen argues that the root of all psychological suffering is thinking itself — not circumstances — and offers a framework for stepping beyond compulsive thought to access deeper wisdom and well-being.

## Key Topics
- How thinking creates and sustains suffering
- Distinguishing between useful thought and compulsive rumination
- Accessing innate wisdom beyond the thinking mind
- Practical approaches to mental freedom

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're caught in overthinking or anxiety and need a quick, powerful reminder that your thoughts are not always truth.
