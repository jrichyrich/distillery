# Choosing Clarity: The Path to Fearlessness
**Author:** Kimberly Giles

## What This Book Is About
Life coach Kimberly Giles presents a framework for overcoming fear and achieving clarity in life decisions. The book provides tools and principles from her ClarityPoint Coaching methodology for moving past fear-based thinking and living with greater purpose and confidence.

## Key Topics
- Overcoming fear and self-doubt
- Clarity-based decision-making frameworks
- Coaching principles for personal growth
- Living with purpose and intention
- Fearlessness as a practice

## When To Reach For This Book
When fear is holding you back from making important life decisions and you need a structured approach to finding clarity.
