# Blindspotting: How to See What's Holding You Back as a Leader
**Author:** Martin Dubin

## What This Book Is About
Published by Harvard Business Review Press, Dubin explores the psychological blind spots that hold leaders back from peak performance. The book provides frameworks for developing greater self-awareness, identifying hidden patterns of behavior, and overcoming the unconscious barriers that limit executive effectiveness.

## Key Topics
- Leadership blind spots and self-deception
- Executive self-awareness development
- Psychological barriers to leadership effectiveness
- Problem-solving frameworks for leaders
- Organizational psychology insights

## When To Reach For This Book
When you sense something is limiting your leadership effectiveness but can't pinpoint what it is, or when coaching executives through development plateaus.
