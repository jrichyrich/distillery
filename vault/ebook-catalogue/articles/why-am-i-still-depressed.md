# Why Am I Still Depressed?
**Author:** Jim Phelps

## What This Book Is About
Why Am I Still Depressed? helps readers recognize and manage Bipolar II and "soft" bipolar disorder -- conditions that are frequently misdiagnosed as standard depression. Dr. Jim Phelps explains why traditional antidepressants may not work for these patients and provides a framework for understanding the bipolar spectrum. The book offers guidance on proper diagnosis, treatment options, and managing the ups and downs of mood disorders.

## Key Topics
- The bipolar spectrum and soft bipolar disorder
- Why Bipolar II is frequently misdiagnosed
- Limitations of antidepressants for bipolar conditions
- Treatment options including mood stabilizers
- Managing mood cycles and recognizing patterns

## When To Reach For This Book
When antidepressants are not helping your depression and you suspect a bipolar spectrum condition may be at play.
