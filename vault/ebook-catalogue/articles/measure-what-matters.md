# Measure What Matters
**Author:** John Doerr

## What This Book Is About
Legendary venture capitalist John Doerr introduces the OKR (Objectives and Key Results) framework that has driven success at Google, the Gates Foundation, Bono's ONE campaign, and many other organizations. The book explains how to set ambitious goals, track progress with measurable key results, and create a culture of accountability and transparency.

## Key Topics
- The OKR framework: objectives and key results
- How Google and other organizations use OKRs
- Setting stretch goals and tracking measurable progress
- Building organizational alignment and transparency
- Case studies from tech, nonprofits, and social enterprise

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a proven goal-setting framework to align your team or organization around what matters most.
