# The Art of Innovation
**Author:** Tom Kelley

## What This Book Is About
Tom Kelley, general manager of the legendary design firm IDEO, reveals the strategies and secrets behind the company's approach to innovation. Through stories from IDEO's work designing products for companies like Apple, P&G, and Samsung, Kelley shows how to foster a culture of creativity, run effective brainstorms, build rapid prototypes, and create breakthrough experiences.

## Key Topics
- IDEO's innovation methodology
- Brainstorming techniques and hot groups
- Rapid prototyping as a thinking tool
- Observational research and human-centered design
- Creating experiences for customers
- Building innovation-friendly organizational culture

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need inspiration and practical methods for fostering innovation in your team or organization.
