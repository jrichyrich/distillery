# The Power of Habit
**Author:** Charles Duhigg

## What This Book Is About
The Power of Habit explores the science of how habits work in individuals, organizations, and societies. Charles Duhigg introduces the "habit loop" -- cue, routine, reward -- and shows how understanding this framework allows us to change our behaviors. Drawing on research from neuroscience, psychology, and business, the book demonstrates how keystone habits can trigger widespread transformation in personal life, companies, and social movements.

## Key Topics
- The habit loop: cue, routine, reward
- How keystone habits create cascading change
- The role of willpower and belief in habit change
- How organizations leverage habits for productivity
- The neuroscience behind automatic behaviors
- Case studies from Alcoa, Starbucks, the civil rights movement, and more

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand the mechanics of habit formation and learn a practical framework for changing behaviors in yourself or your organization.
