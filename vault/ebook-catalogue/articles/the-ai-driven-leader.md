# The AI-Driven Leader
**Author:** Geoff Woods

## What This Book Is About
A guide for leaders on harnessing artificial intelligence to make faster, smarter strategic decisions. Woods provides frameworks for integrating AI into leadership workflows, covering how executives can leverage AI tools for better decision-making without needing deep technical expertise.

## Key Topics
- AI applications for executive decision-making
- Strategic thinking augmented by AI
- Practical AI adoption for business leaders
- Digital transformation leadership
- AI thought leadership principles

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want practical frameworks for incorporating AI into your leadership and strategic decision-making processes.
