# Team of Teams
**Author:** Stanley McChrystal, Tantum Collins, David Silverman, and Chris Fussell

## What This Book Is About
Team of Teams draws on General McChrystal's experience commanding the Joint Special Operations Task Force in Iraq to argue that traditional hierarchical organizations are too slow for today's complex, fast-moving environments. McChrystal shows how he transformed a rigid military bureaucracy into an agile network of teams that shared information and decision-making authority. The book applies these military lessons to business and organizational leadership.

## Key Topics
- Why traditional command-and-control structures fail in complexity
- Creating shared consciousness across an organization
- Empowered execution and decentralized decision-making
- Building trust and transparency at scale
- Adapting organizations to rapidly changing environments

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to transform a large, siloed organization into one that can adapt and respond quickly to complex challenges.
