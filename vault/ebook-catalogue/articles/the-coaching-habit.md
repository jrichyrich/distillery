# The Coaching Habit: Say Less, Ask More & Change the Way You Lead Forever
**Author:** Michael Bungay Stanier

## What This Book Is About
Stanier distills the art of coaching into seven essential questions that any manager or leader can use to be more coach-like. The core principle: stay curious a little longer and rush to action and advice-giving a little more slowly. Endorsed by Daniel Pink and Brene Brown, the book focuses on building the habit of asking better questions rather than providing answers.

## Key Topics
- Seven essential coaching questions
- Building a coaching habit as a leader
- Staying curious vs. rushing to advice
- Practical coaching frameworks for managers
- Habit formation for leadership behavior change
- Empowering others through questions

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to become a more effective leader by learning to coach your team through questions rather than directives.
