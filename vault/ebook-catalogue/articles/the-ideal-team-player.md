# The Ideal Team Player
**Author:** Patrick Lencioni

## What This Book Is About
The Ideal Team Player, told as a leadership fable, identifies the three essential virtues that make someone a great team player: humble, hungry, and smart (people smart). Lencioni, author of The Five Dysfunctions of a Team, provides a practical framework for recognizing, cultivating, and hiring people who embody these traits. The book includes a model for assessing candidates and developing existing team members.

## Key Topics
- The three virtues: humble, hungry, and smart
- How to identify ideal team players during hiring
- Developing the three virtues in existing employees
- Why missing even one virtue creates problems
- Applying the model to build stronger teams

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a clear, actionable framework for hiring and developing people who work well on teams.
