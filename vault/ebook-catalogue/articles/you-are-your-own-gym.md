# You Are Your Own Gym
**Author:** Mark Lauren and Joshua Clark

## What This Book Is About
You Are Your Own Gym presents a complete bodyweight training program developed by Mark Lauren, a Military Physical Training Specialist who prepared nearly a thousand trainees for Special Operations. The book proves you do not need a gym or equipment to get in elite shape, providing exercises and programs that use only your own body weight. It includes routines for all fitness levels based on the same principles used to train special forces operators.

## Key Topics
- Bodyweight exercise progressions for all levels
- Military-grade fitness without equipment
- Strength, endurance, and flexibility training
- Program design and workout scheduling
- Nutrition fundamentals for body composition

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a proven, no-equipment fitness program you can do anywhere.
