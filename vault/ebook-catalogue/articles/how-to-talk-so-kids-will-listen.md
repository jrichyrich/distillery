# How to Talk So Kids Will Listen & Listen So Kids Will Talk
**Author:** Adele Faber, Elaine Mazlish

## What This Book Is About
The bestselling parenting classic (30th anniversary edition) that teaches respectful, effective communication techniques for parents. Faber and Mazlish provide practical skills — illustrated with cartoons — for dealing with children's negative feelings, engaging cooperation without nagging, using alternatives to punishment, resolving conflicts, and building self-esteem, all while maintaining a loving relationship.

## Key Topics
- Acknowledging and validating children's emotions
- Engaging cooperation without threats or bribes
- Alternatives to punishment that preserve goodwill
- Encouraging autonomy and building self-esteem
- Resolving family conflicts peacefully
- Expressing anger without being hurtful

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're struggling with communication breakdowns with your kids and want practical, respectful techniques that actually work.
