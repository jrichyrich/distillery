# A Whack on the Side of the Head
**Author:** Roger Von Oech

## What This Book Is About
A Whack on the Side of the Head is a classic guide to unlocking creativity by breaking through the mental locks that keep us thinking in conventional patterns. Von Oech identifies ten mental blocks to creative thinking and provides exercises, provocations, and techniques to break free of each one. The book has been a staple of innovation training for decades and encourages playful, unconventional approaches to problem-solving.

## Key Topics
- Ten mental locks that block creative thinking
- Techniques for breaking out of conventional patterns
- The role of play and humor in creativity
- Challenging assumptions and sacred cows
- Practical exercises for generating new ideas

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel stuck in conventional thinking and need a creative jolt to approach problems differently.
