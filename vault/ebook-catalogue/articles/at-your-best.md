# At Your Best
**Author:** Carey Nieuwhof

## What This Book Is About
Nieuwhof makes the case that burnout is not inevitable and provides a system for managing time and energy so that you do your best work when you're at your best. Endorsed by Adam Grant, Greg McKeown, Cal Newport, and Seth Godin, the book offers a practical framework for aligning your calendar with your priorities and achieving more while staying healthy.

## Key Topics
- Time and energy management systems
- Burnout prevention and recovery
- Aligning priorities with calendar
- Peak performance scheduling
- Work-life integration strategies

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel burned out or sense that your calendar doesn't reflect your actual priorities, and you need a practical system for reclaiming your time.
