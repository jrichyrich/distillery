# Good Energy
**Author:** Casey Means, Calley Means

## What This Book Is About
Dr. Casey Means makes the case that metabolic dysfunction is the root cause of most chronic diseases — from diabetes and heart disease to depression and Alzheimer's. The book explains how mitochondrial health and blood sugar regulation are foundational to overall wellness, and provides a practical roadmap for optimizing metabolic health through food, movement, sleep, and environmental changes.

## Key Topics
- Metabolic health as the foundation of overall health
- How insulin resistance drives chronic disease
- Mitochondrial function and cellular energy
- The role of food systems and regenerative agriculture
- Practical strategies for blood sugar optimization
- The broken incentives in modern healthcare

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand the metabolic root causes of chronic disease and take actionable steps toward better cellular energy and health.
