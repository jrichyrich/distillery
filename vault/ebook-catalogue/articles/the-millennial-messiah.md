# The Millennial Messiah
**Author:** Bruce R. McConkie

## What This Book Is About
The crowning work of Elder Bruce R. McConkie's trilogy on the life of Christ, this comprehensive doctrinal study examines every aspect of the Second Coming from a Latter-day Saint perspective. Drawing from all four standard works and inspired prophets, McConkie explores the signs of the times, the gathering of Israel, the role of the Book of Mormon, and the events that will precede and accompany Christ's return.

## Key Topics
- Signs and events preceding the Second Coming
- The gathering of Israel and the Ten Tribes
- The role of the Book of Mormon in the last days
- The two prophets in Jerusalem
- Millennial prophecy and the reign of Christ

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want an exhaustive Latter-day Saint doctrinal treatment of the Second Coming and last-days prophecy.
