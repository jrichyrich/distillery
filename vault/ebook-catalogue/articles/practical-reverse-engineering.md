# Practical Reverse Engineering
**Author:** Bruce Dang, Alexandre Gazet & Elias Bachaalany

## What This Book Is About
Practical Reverse Engineering is a hands-on guide to reverse engineering x86, x64, and ARM architectures, with deep coverage of the Windows kernel, debugging tools, and obfuscation techniques. The book takes readers from foundational assembly and instruction sets through real-world walk-throughs of driver analysis and malware deobfuscation, with exercises throughout to build practical skills.

## Key Topics
- x86 and x64 architecture, registers, and instruction sets
- ARM architecture and instruction set fundamentals
- Windows kernel internals: drivers, IRPs, and system mechanisms
- Debugging tools, scripting, and automation
- Obfuscation and deobfuscation techniques
- Hands-on walk-throughs and exercises

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to develop or sharpen practical reverse engineering skills across multiple architectures and platforms.
