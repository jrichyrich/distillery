# The Ketamine Papers
**Author:** Phil Wolfson, M.D.

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive collection of essays and research on the therapeutic use of ketamine for depression, trauma, and personal transformation. Dr. Wolfson brings together diverse perspectives on ketamine's applications, dosages, and routes of administration, exploring both its healing potential and its limitations as a tool for mental health treatment and expanded consciousness.

## Key Topics
- Ketamine as a treatment for depression and trauma
- Clinical applications, dosages, and administration routes
- Psychedelic-assisted psychotherapy
- Consciousness expansion and ego dissolution
- Risks, limitations, and individual variability

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a thorough scientific and therapeutic overview of ketamine's role in modern mental health treatment.
