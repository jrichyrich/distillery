# The Law of Love
**Author:** Steve Young

## What This Book Is About
NFL Hall of Famer Steve Young explores the transformative power of Christlike love as the foundational commandment. Drawing on scripture, personal experience, and the teachings of Latter-day Saint leaders, Young argues that loving as God loves -- seeking another's healing and expecting nothing in return -- is the highest law and the key to meaningful relationships.

## Key Topics
- The great commandment to love
- Christlike love as healing and selfless service
- Faith and family relationships
- Scriptural foundations of divine love
- Applying the law of love in daily life

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want an inspiring, faith-centered exploration of what it means to love unconditionally.
