# Toxic Parents
**Author:** Susan Forward and Craig Buck

## What This Book Is About
Toxic Parents identifies the patterns of destructive parenting -- from inadequacy and control to verbal, physical, and sexual abuse -- and provides a roadmap for adult children to overcome their hurtful legacies. Forward categorizes toxic parent types and shows how childhood experiences shape adult behavior, relationships, and self-worth. The book emphasizes that while you are not to blame for what happened, you can take action to reclaim your life.

## Key Topics
- Types of toxic parents (controllers, alcoholics, abusers)
- How childhood abuse shapes adult behavior
- Breaking free from guilt and manipulation
- Confrontation and boundary-setting strategies
- Healing from the legacy of destructive parenting

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to understand and heal from the lasting effects of a dysfunctional or abusive upbringing.
