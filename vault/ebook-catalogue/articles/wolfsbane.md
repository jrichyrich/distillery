# Wolfsbane
**Author:** Guy Haley

## What This Book Is About
Wolfsbane is Book 49 in the Horus Heresy series, focusing on Leman Russ, Primarch of the Space Wolves, as he sets out on a mission to kill Horus himself. The novel explores the Wolf King's character, his relationship with his brothers, and his determination to end the Heresy by cutting off the head of the serpent. It is a key entry in the series as the loyalist Primarchs begin their counterattack.

## Key Topics
- Leman Russ's mission to assassinate Horus
- Space Wolves Legion lore and culture
- The escalating Horus Heresy conflict
- Brotherhood and duty among the Primarchs
- The cost of war and vengeance

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are following the Horus Heresy series and want to see the Space Wolves enter the fray against Horus.
