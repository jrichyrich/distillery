# Strategic Problem Solving
**Author:** Mark Hartley

## What This Book Is About
Strategic Problem Solving is a step-by-step guide to tackling hard problems using data analysis, critical thinking, and structured frameworks. Mark Hartley provides a methodology for breaking down complex challenges, making smart decisions, driving innovation, and leading with confidence. The book is aimed at professionals and leaders who want to improve their ability to diagnose problems systematically and arrive at effective solutions.

## Key Topics
- Structured problem-solving methodologies
- Leveraging data analysis for better decisions
- Critical thinking techniques and cognitive bias awareness
- Driving innovation through systematic problem solving
- Leading teams through complex challenges
- Practical frameworks and step-by-step processes

## When To Reach For This Book
When you face a complex problem at work and want a structured, repeatable approach to analyzing it and driving toward a solution.
