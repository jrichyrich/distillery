# Cybersecurity First Principles: A Reboot of Strategy and Tactics
**Author:** Rick Howard

## What This Book Is About
Rick Howard rethinks cybersecurity from the ground up using first principles thinking. The book identifies the atomic cybersecurity first principle and derives five essential strategies: Zero Trust, Intrusion Kill Chain Prevention, Resilience, Risk Forecasting, and Automation. Each strategy is broken down into specific tactics with practical implementation guidance.

## Key Topics
- First principles approach to cybersecurity
- Zero Trust philosophy and implementation
- Intrusion Kill Chain prevention (Lockheed Martin model)
- Resilience strategy and business continuity
- Risk forecasting and automation
- Cyber threat intelligence operations
- Identity management, SSO, and MFA tactics
- Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to build or restructure a cybersecurity program from first principles, or when you want a strategic framework that connects high-level strategy to specific tactical implementations.
