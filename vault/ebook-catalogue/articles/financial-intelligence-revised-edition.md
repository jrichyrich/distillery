# Financial Intelligence: A Manager's Guide to Knowing What the Numbers Really Mean
**Author:** Karen Berman, Joe Knight

## What This Book Is About
A practical guide to financial literacy for non-financial managers, published by Harvard Business Review Press. Berman and Knight demystify income statements, balance sheets, cash flow, and ROI — teaching managers to understand the numbers behind business decisions, ask the right questions, and use financial data to drive better outcomes without needing an accounting degree.

## Key Topics
- Reading and interpreting income statements and balance sheets
- Understanding cash flow vs. profit
- Financial ratios and what they reveal
- The art and subjectivity behind financial numbers
- Using financial intelligence for better business decisions

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're a manager who needs to understand financial statements, participate in budget discussions, or make data-driven business decisions.
