# Essential Guide to the GDPR
**Author:** TrustArc

## What This Book Is About
A practical guide that distills the 200+ page EU General Data Protection Regulation into a five-phase compliance roadmap. Designed for professionals across legal, IT, and business functions, it covers building a privacy program, conducting data inventories, managing consent, performing data protection impact assessments, and demonstrating ongoing compliance.

## Key Topics
- Five-phase GDPR compliance roadmap
- Data inventory and data flow analysis
- Consent management and individual rights
- Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs)
- Data breach incident response planning
- Third-party data transfer management

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a concise, actionable overview of GDPR compliance steps without reading the full regulation.
