# Mindset
**Author:** Carol S. Dweck

## What This Book Is About
Stanford psychologist Carol Dweck reveals the power of the growth mindset versus the fixed mindset. Through decades of research, she shows how our beliefs about our own abilities profoundly affect every area of life -- from education and work to relationships and parenting -- and demonstrates that talent alone does not determine success; the belief that abilities can be developed does.

## Key Topics
- Fixed mindset versus growth mindset
- How mindset affects learning, achievement, and resilience
- Mindset in sports, business, and relationships
- Parenting and teaching with a growth mindset
- Changing your mindset through awareness and practice

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand why some people thrive on challenge while others collapse, and how to shift your own orientation toward growth.
