# The Greatness Mindset
**Author:** Lewis Howes

## What This Book Is About
Howes provides a framework for reconnecting with your purpose, overcoming self-doubt, and unlocking inner strength in a world overcome with burnout, stress, and anxiety. The book offers practical tools to break through limiting beliefs, define a meaningful mission, and use your unique gifts to create impact — drawing on interviews with thought leaders and Howes's own journey from adversity to success.

## Key Topics
- Identifying and overcoming limiting beliefs
- Defining your meaningful mission and purpose
- Building a greatness mindset to overcome self-doubt
- Practical frameworks for personal transformation
- Using gifts and strengths to serve others

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel stuck by self-doubt or lack of direction and need actionable frameworks to reconnect with your purpose.
