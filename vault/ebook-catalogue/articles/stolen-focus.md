# Stolen Focus
**Author:** Johann Hari

## What This Book Is About
Stolen Focus investigates the global attention crisis, arguing that our ability to focus is being systematically eroded by powerful forces -- from social media and technology design to stress, poor diet, and environmental factors. Johann Hari interviews scientists, activists, and tech insiders to identify twelve key causes of the attention collapse, making the case that this is not primarily a personal failing but a structural problem requiring collective solutions.

## Key Topics
- The twelve causes of the attention crisis
- How technology and social media are designed to fragment focus
- The impact of surveillance capitalism on attention
- Stress, sleep, and diet as attention destroyers
- The decline of deep reading and sustained thinking
- Individual and systemic solutions to reclaim focus

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand why it has become so hard to focus and what can be done about it at both the personal and societal level.
