# Michael Vey 7: The Final Spark
**Author:** Richard Paul Evans

## What This Book Is About
The seventh and final installment in the Michael Vey series, concluding the saga of a teenager with electrical powers who battles the evil Hatch and the Elgen corporation. Michael and his friends face their ultimate showdown in this climactic adventure that resolves the series' central conflicts.

## Key Topics
- The final battle against the Elgen corporation
- Friendship, sacrifice, and courage
- Superpowers and their consequences
- Good versus evil in a young adult adventure

## When To Reach For This Book
When you have followed the Michael Vey series and want to see how it all ends.
