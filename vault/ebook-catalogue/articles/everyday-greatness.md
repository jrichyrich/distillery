# Everyday Greatness
**Author:** Stephen R. Covey

## What This Book Is About
A collection of inspiring stories curated by Stephen Covey from Reader's Digest, organized around the principles of character-driven living. Covey uses these real-life stories to illustrate how ordinary people achieve everyday greatness through integrity, courage, kindness, and commitment to principles — showing that greatness isn't about fame but about how you live each day.

## Key Topics
- Character-based principles for daily life
- Inspiring real-life stories of ordinary greatness
- Integrity, contribution, and personal responsibility
- Covey's principle-centered leadership applied to daily living

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a quick dose of inspiration and a reminder that greatness is found in small, principled daily choices.
