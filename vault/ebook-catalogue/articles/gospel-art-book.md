# Gospel Art Book
**Author:** The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

## What This Book Is About
A collection of artwork depicting scenes from the Old Testament, New Testament, Book of Mormon, and Church history, published by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Used for teaching, study, and personal devotion, the images span from the Creation through the Restoration and are a standard visual reference in Latter-day Saint congregations.

## Key Topics
- Biblical and Book of Mormon scripture illustrations
- Life and ministry of Jesus Christ
- Old Testament and New Testament scenes
- Church history and Restoration artwork

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need visual references for scripture stories or gospel teaching.
