# Is God Disappointed in Me?
**Author:** Kurt Francom

## What This Book Is About
The host of the Leading Saints podcast tackles the pervasive feeling of divine disappointment and shame that many people of faith carry. Part memoir and part sermon, Francom argues that God's relationship with us is rooted in love and healing rather than disappointment, offering a compassionate reframing of what it means to be "enough" in the eyes of God.

## Key Topics
- Overcoming shame in a religious context
- Understanding grace versus perfectionism
- God's love as a healing force
- Reframing motivation from fear to love
- Practical spirituality for everyday believers

## When To Reach For This Book
When you struggle with feelings of spiritual inadequacy and want a compassionate, faith-based perspective on grace.
