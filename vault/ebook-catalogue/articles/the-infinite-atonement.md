# The Infinite Atonement
**Author:** Tad R. Callister

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive exploration of the Atonement of Jesus Christ from a Latter-day Saint theological perspective. Callister examines the scriptural, doctrinal, and philosophical dimensions of the Atonement, explaining why it is infinite in scope and what that means for every individual seeking redemption and grace.

## Key Topics
- The infinite nature and scope of the Atonement
- Scriptural foundations across LDS standard works
- The role of grace, mercy, and justice
- How the Atonement applies to individual lives
- Comparative Christian theology on redemption

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a deep doctrinal study of the Atonement from a Latter-day Saint perspective.
