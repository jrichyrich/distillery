# Loserthink
**Author:** Scott Adams

## What This Book Is About
Dilbert creator Scott Adams identifies the thinking errors he calls "loserthink" -- the unproductive mental habits people fall into when they lack training in key disciplines. He walks through how psychologists, artists, historians, engineers, leaders, scientists, entrepreneurs, and economists think, showing how adopting these mental frameworks can help anyone break out of cognitive traps.

## Key Topics
- Thinking like a psychologist, engineer, scientist, and economist
- Identifying and escaping mental prisons
- Cognitive biases in political and public discourse
- Mental models from multiple disciplines
- Breaking others out of unproductive thinking patterns

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to expand your mental toolkit by learning how different disciplines approach problems.
