# Leadership and Self-Deception
**Author:** The Arbinger Institute

## What This Book Is About
Through a compelling narrative, the Arbinger Institute reveals how self-deception -- the inability to see that we are part of the problems we complain about -- undermines leadership, teamwork, and relationships. The book introduces the concept of being "in the box" and provides a framework for getting out of it to become a more effective leader and human being.

## Key Topics
- Self-deception and its impact on leadership
- The "in the box" and "out of the box" framework
- How self-betrayal leads to self-justification
- Transforming organizational culture through personal change
- Building authentic relationships at work and home

## When To Reach For This Book
When you sense that interpersonal conflicts at work or home keep repeating and suspect the common denominator might be you.
