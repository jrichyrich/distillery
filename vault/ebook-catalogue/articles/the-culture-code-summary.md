# The Culture Code (Summary)
**Author:** Daniel Coyle

## What This Book Is About
This is a summary of Daniel Coyle's The Culture Code, which explores the secrets behind why certain groups thrive while others fail. Coyle identifies three key skills that generate group cohesion and cooperation: building safety, sharing vulnerability, and establishing purpose. The book draws on research from elite teams including Navy SEALs, Pixar, and the San Antonio Spurs.

## Key Topics
- Building psychological safety within teams
- The role of vulnerability in creating trust
- Establishing shared purpose and direction
- Signals that foster belonging and connection
- How high-performing cultures are built and maintained

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a quick overview of the cultural dynamics that make teams successful.
