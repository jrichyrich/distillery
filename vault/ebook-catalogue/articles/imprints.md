# Imprints
**Author:** Rachel Ann Nunes

## What This Book Is About
When a young girl named Victoria vanishes, her desperate parents turn to Autumn Rain, a woman who can receive emotional impressions from objects treasured by their owners. Together with widowed private detective Ethan McConnell, Autumn's investigation sets off a chain of events that endangers her own life and those she loves most.

## Key Topics
- Psychic impressions and clairvoyance as a story device
- Missing persons investigation
- Suspense and personal danger
- Faith-informed fiction

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a suspenseful mystery with a supernatural twist and faith-based undertones.
