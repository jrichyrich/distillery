# How Will You Measure Your Life?
**Author:** Clayton M. Christensen, James Allworth, Karen Dillon

## What This Book Is About
Harvard Business School professor Clayton Christensen applies some of the most powerful theories from business — disruption, incentive theory, strategy — to the most personal questions of life: How can I be happy in my career? How can I build fulfilling relationships? How can I stay out of jail? The book offers a framework for making life decisions with the same rigor we apply to business strategy.

## Key Topics
- Applying business strategy theories to personal life
- Finding genuine happiness and motivation in your career
- Investing in relationships before it's too late
- The dangers of marginal thinking ("just this once")
- Defining and living by your life's purpose

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to step back from the daily grind and think strategically about whether your life is heading toward what matters most to you.
