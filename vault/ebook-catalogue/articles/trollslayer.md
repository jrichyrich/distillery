# Trollslayer
**Author:** William King

## What This Book Is About
Trollslayer is the first novel in the Gotrek and Felix series set in the Warhammer Fantasy universe. It follows Gotrek Gurnisson, a dwarf Slayer seeking a glorious death in battle, and his reluctant human companion Felix Jaeger, who has sworn to record Gotrek's doom. The book is a collection of interconnected adventures as the duo travel the Old World battling trolls, skaven, and other monstrous threats.

## Key Topics
- Gotrek and Felix's adventures in the Old World
- The Dwarf Slayer oath and quest for doom
- Battles against trolls, beastmen, and chaos
- Warhammer Fantasy world-building and lore
- The bond between unlikely companions

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a fast-paced, grimdark fantasy adventure in the Warhammer universe.
