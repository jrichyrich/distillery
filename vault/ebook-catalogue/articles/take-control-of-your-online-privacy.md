# Take Control of Your Online Privacy
**Author:** Joe Kissell

## What This Book Is About
Take Control of Your Online Privacy is a practical guide to protecting your personal information in the digital age. Kissell walks readers through essential steps for securing their online presence, with a strong emphasis on password management and practical privacy habits. The book was produced in partnership with AgileBits (makers of 1Password) and provides actionable advice for everyday users.

## Key Topics
- Password management best practices
- Protecting personal data online
- Privacy tools and browser settings
- Managing your digital footprint
- Practical steps for internet safety

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a clear, step-by-step guide to improving your personal online privacy and security.
