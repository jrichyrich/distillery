# From Heatmaps to Histograms: A Practical Guide to Cyber Risk Quantification
**Author:** Tony Martin-Vegue

## What This Book Is About
A practical guide to moving beyond subjective, color-coded risk heatmaps toward rigorous, data-driven cyber risk quantification. Martin-Vegue provides frameworks and techniques for measuring cybersecurity risk in financial terms, enabling better communication with executives and boards, more rational resource allocation, and defensible risk decisions.

## Key Topics
- Why qualitative risk heatmaps fail and mislead
- Quantitative risk analysis methods for cybersecurity
- Expressing cyber risk in financial and probabilistic terms
- Building a risk quantification program from scratch
- Communicating risk to executives and boards with data

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to move your cyber risk program from subjective red/yellow/green ratings to defensible, quantitative risk measurement.
