# How the Mighty Fall: And Why Some Companies Never Give In
**Author:** Jim Collins

## What This Book Is About
Collins identifies five stages of corporate decline — from the hubris born of success through the undisciplined pursuit of more, denial of risk, grasping for salvation, and finally capitulation to irrelevance or death. Drawing on research into companies that fell from greatness, he shows that decline is largely self-inflicted and, if caught early enough, can be reversed.

## Key Topics
- Five stages of corporate decline
- Hubris born of success as the first warning sign
- The undisciplined pursuit of more
- Denial of risk and peril
- Grasping for salvation vs. disciplined recovery
- How some companies recover from the brink

## When To Reach For This Book
When you suspect an organization is in decline and need a diagnostic framework to identify which stage it's in and whether recovery is still possible.
