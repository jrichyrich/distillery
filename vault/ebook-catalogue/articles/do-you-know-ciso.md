# Do You Know CISO?
**Author:** Secure Anchor

## What This Book Is About
This short ebook explains everything you need to know about the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) role -- what the position entails, what makes a good CISO, and how to ensure success in the role. It covers the evolution of the CISO from a niche technical position to a vital C-level executive responsible for privacy, data protection, and enterprise-wide security strategy.

## Key Topics
- The definition and scope of the CISO role
- Key qualities and skills of an effective CISO
- The evolution of the CISO as a C-suite executive
- Hiring considerations for organizations seeking a CISO
- How CISOs drive security strategy and board engagement

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a quick primer on the CISO role, whether you are considering becoming one, hiring one, or working with one.
