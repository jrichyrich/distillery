# Potatoes Not Prozac
**Author:** Kathleen DesMaisons

## What This Book Is About
Potatoes Not Prozac presents a natural, seven-step dietary plan for people who are sugar-sensitive -- those whose brain chemistry makes them particularly vulnerable to sugar cravings, mood swings, and addictive behaviors. Dr. DesMaisons explains how certain foods affect serotonin and beta-endorphin levels, and provides a structured program to stabilize blood sugar and brain chemistry through food choices rather than medication.

## Key Topics
- Sugar sensitivity and its biochemical basis
- How food affects serotonin and beta-endorphins
- The seven-step dietary plan for controlling cravings
- The relationship between sugar, mood, and addiction
- Using complex carbohydrates to stabilize brain chemistry
- Building sustainable eating habits

## When To Reach For This Book
When you struggle with sugar cravings, mood swings, or emotional eating and want a nutrition-based approach to restoring balance.
