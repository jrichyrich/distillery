# Charting the Book of Mormon: Visual Aids for Personal Study and Teaching
**Author:** John W. Welch & J. Gregory Welch

## What This Book Is About
A collection of visual charts and diagrams designed to enhance personal study and teaching of the Book of Mormon. Published through BYU's Maxwell Institute (FARMS), the charts cover geography, timelines, literary structures, doctrinal themes, and authorship patterns, making complex scriptural relationships visually accessible.

## Key Topics
- Visual charts of Book of Mormon timelines and geography
- Literary and structural analysis diagrams
- Doctrinal theme mapping
- Teaching aids for scripture study
- FARMS/Maxwell Institute scholarship

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need visual aids to enhance your study or teaching of the Book of Mormon, or when you want to see complex scriptural relationships laid out graphically.
