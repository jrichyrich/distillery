# When Your Child Has an Eating Disorder
**Author:** Abigail H. Natenshon

## What This Book Is About
When Your Child Has an Eating Disorder is a step-by-step workbook for parents and caregivers navigating a child's eating disorder. Natenshon provides practical guidance for recognizing warning signs, understanding the psychological roots of eating disorders, supporting recovery, and maintaining family stability during treatment. The book empowers parents to become active, informed participants in their child's healing.

## Key Topics
- Recognizing signs of anorexia, bulimia, and other eating disorders
- Understanding the psychology behind eating disorders
- How to support your child through treatment and recovery
- Maintaining family health during a child's illness
- Step-by-step exercises and practical tools for caregivers

## When To Reach For This Book
When your child is struggling with an eating disorder and you need practical guidance for supporting their recovery.
