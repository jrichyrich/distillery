# The Complete Guide to Fasting
**Author:** Jason Fung & Jimmy Moore

## What This Book Is About
Nephrologist Dr. Jason Fung and health podcaster Jimmy Moore provide a comprehensive guide to therapeutic fasting, covering intermittent, alternate-day, and extended fasting protocols. The book explains the science behind fasting's effects on insulin, metabolism, and disease prevention, with practical advice for safely incorporating fasting into your life.

## Key Topics
- Intermittent and extended fasting protocols
- The science of insulin resistance and metabolic health
- Fasting for weight loss and disease prevention
- Practical fasting schedules and tips
- Safety considerations and common concerns
- Fasting and diabetes management

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a science-backed, practical guide to starting or deepening a fasting practice for health benefits.
