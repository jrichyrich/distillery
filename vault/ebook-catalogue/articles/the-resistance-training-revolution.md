# The Resistance Training Revolution
**Author:** Sal Di Stefano

## What This Book Is About
The Resistance Training Revolution makes the case that resistance training -- not cardio -- is the most effective form of exercise for fat loss, metabolic health, and long-term well-being. Sal Di Stefano, host of the Mind Pump podcast, draws on exercise science to explain why building muscle transforms your body's metabolism, and provides practical workout programs for beginners through advanced lifters.

## Key Topics
- Why resistance training outperforms cardio for fat loss
- The metabolic advantages of building lean muscle
- How to structure effective strength training programs
- Common exercise myths and misconceptions
- Programming for different fitness levels and goals
- The role of nutrition in supporting resistance training

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand why strength training should be the foundation of your fitness routine and need practical programming to get started.
