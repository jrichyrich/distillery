# The Great Mental Models, Volume 1: General Thinking Concepts
**Author:** Shane Parrish, Rhiannon Beaubien

## What This Book Is About
From the creator of Farnam Street, this book catalogs the most useful general thinking concepts — mental models — that help you understand how the world works and make better decisions. Volume 1 covers foundational models like the map is not the territory, first principles thinking, inversion, Occam's razor, and Hanlon's razor, with real-world examples showing how each model applies.

## Key Topics
- The map is not the territory
- First principles thinking
- Inversion and second-order thinking
- Circle of competence
- Occam's razor and Hanlon's razor
- Thought experiments and probabilistic thinking

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to build a toolkit of mental models for clearer thinking and better decision-making across any domain.
