# The Law of Happiness
**Author:** Henry Cloud

## What This Book Is About
Psychologist and bestselling author Henry Cloud bridges spiritual wisdom and modern positive psychology research to reveal the principles that lead to lasting happiness. He explores how practices rooted in faith and backed by science can transform your emotional well-being and daily experience of life.

## Key Topics
- The intersection of faith and positive psychology
- Evidence-based practices for lasting happiness
- Spiritual disciplines that promote well-being
- Overcoming barriers to joy and contentment
- Practical steps for a happier life

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a concise, faith-informed guide to happiness grounded in both scripture and science.
