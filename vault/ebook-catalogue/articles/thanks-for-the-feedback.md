# Thanks for the Feedback
**Author:** Douglas Stone and Sheila Heen

## What This Book Is About
Thanks for the Feedback flips the traditional approach to feedback by focusing not on how to give it, but on how to receive it well. Stone and Heen, authors of Difficult Conversations, present a framework for understanding why feedback triggers defensiveness and how to extract value from even poorly delivered feedback. The book identifies three types of feedback triggers and provides strategies for managing each.

## Key Topics
- The three feedback triggers (truth, relationship, identity)
- Separating appreciation, coaching, and evaluation
- Managing emotional reactions to feedback
- Building self-awareness through feedback
- Using feedback to drive growth and improvement

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to get better at receiving feedback without becoming defensive, whether from bosses, partners, or family.
