# You Are Receiving Revelation, Now Act
**Author:** Loren G. Dalton

## What This Book Is About
You Are Receiving Revelation, Now Act encourages readers to recognize and act on personal revelation in their daily lives. Written from a Latter-day Saint perspective, Dalton helps readers understand how the Holy Ghost communicates, how to distinguish divine promptings from other influences, and how to develop the courage and faith to act on the guidance they receive.

## Key Topics
- Recognizing personal revelation
- How the Holy Ghost communicates
- Distinguishing revelation from other influences
- Building faith to act on spiritual promptings
- Practical application of revelation in daily life

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to deepen your ability to receive and act on personal revelation.
