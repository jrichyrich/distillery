# Ten Days to Self-Esteem
**Author:** David D. Burns

## What This Book Is About
Ten Days to Self-Esteem is a structured, ten-step program based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) principles from the author of Feeling Good. Burns guides readers through practical exercises to identify and change negative thought patterns that fuel low self-esteem, bad moods, and procrastination. The workbook format includes self-help forms, charts, and the acceptance paradox technique.

## Key Topics
- The connection between thoughts and feelings
- Techniques for breaking out of bad moods
- The acceptance paradox for self-esteem
- Overcoming perfectionism and procrastination
- Root causes of negative self-image
- Practice-based CBT exercises

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a structured, hands-on CBT workbook to improve your self-esteem and break negative thinking patterns.
