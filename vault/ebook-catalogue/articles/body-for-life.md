# Body for Life
**Author:** Bill Phillips & Michael D'Orso

## What This Book Is About
A bestselling 12-week fitness and nutrition program that promises to transform both body and mind. Bill Phillips provides a structured plan combining weight training, cardio, and nutrition with motivational principles, supported by real-life transformation stories from everyday people including a NASA engineer and physicians.

## Key Topics
- 12-week body transformation program
- Strength training and cardio programming
- Nutrition planning and meal structure
- Mental toughness and motivation
- Real-life transformation case studies
- Mind-body connection in fitness

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a structured, time-bound fitness program that addresses both the physical and mental aspects of body transformation.
