# Reset: How to Change What's Not Working
**Author:** Dan Heath

## What This Book Is About
Reset explores how to fix broken systems, processes, and situations by identifying the leverage points where change is most effective. Dan Heath, co-author of Switch and Made to Stick, applies his signature storytelling and research-driven approach to show how individuals and organizations can move past frustration and redesign what is not working -- from workplace dysfunction to personal habits to community problems.

## Key Topics
- Identifying what is truly broken versus symptoms of deeper issues
- Finding leverage points for meaningful change
- Redesigning systems and processes
- Overcoming inertia and resistance to change
- Practical frameworks for diagnosing and fixing problems

## When To Reach For This Book
When something in your work or life clearly is not working and you need a structured approach to diagnosing the real problem and making effective change.
