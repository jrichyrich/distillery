# The Obesity Code
**Author:** Jason Fung, M.D.

## What This Book Is About
Nephrologist Dr. Jason Fung challenges the conventional calories-in/calories-out model of obesity, arguing that insulin resistance is the root cause of weight gain. He synthesizes decades of research to explain why diets fail, how hormonal imbalances drive fat storage, and why intermittent fasting is a powerful tool for resetting metabolism and achieving lasting weight loss.

## Key Topics
- Insulin resistance as the primary driver of obesity
- Why calorie counting and exercise alone fail
- The hormonal model of weight gain
- Intermittent fasting as a metabolic reset
- The role of sugar, refined carbohydrates, and processed food

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand the science behind why diets fail and how to address the hormonal root causes of weight gain.
