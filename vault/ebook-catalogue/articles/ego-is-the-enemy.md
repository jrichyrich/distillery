# Ego Is the Enemy
**Author:** Ryan Holiday

## What This Book Is About
Holiday draws on historical and contemporary examples to show how ego — the unhealthy belief in our own importance — is the single greatest obstacle to success at every stage: aspiring, succeeding, and failing. The book argues that replacing ego with humility, disciplined action, and continuous learning is what separates those who achieve lasting greatness from those who flame out.

## Key Topics
- How ego undermines aspiration, success, and recovery from failure
- Historical examples of ego-driven downfall and humble greatness
- The discipline of staying a student and doing the work
- Managing success without letting it corrupt your judgment
- Resilience through humility after setbacks

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel ego creeping in — after a big win, during a pursuit of status, or when you need a reminder that humility is a competitive advantage.
