# Living a Century or More
**Author:** William Cortvriendt, M.D.

## What This Book Is About
A physician's evidence-based guide to extending lifespan and healthspan. Dr. Cortvriendt synthesizes scientific research on aging, nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle factors to help readers make informed decisions about their health and improve their chances of living past 100 with vitality.

## Key Topics
- The science of aging and longevity
- Evidence-based nutrition for a longer life
- Exercise and lifestyle factors that extend healthspan
- Preventive health strategies
- Making informed health decisions

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a science-backed overview of what actually works for living a longer, healthier life.
