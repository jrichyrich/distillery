# Effective DevOps
**Author:** Jennifer Davis, Katherine Daniels

## What This Book Is About
Published by O'Reilly, this book goes beyond tools and automation to focus on building a DevOps culture of collaboration, affinity, and shared responsibility at scale. Davis and Daniels argue that effective DevOps is primarily about people and culture — breaking down silos between development and operations teams to deliver software faster and more reliably.

## Key Topics
- Building a culture of collaboration between dev and ops
- Scaling DevOps practices across large organizations
- Tooling and automation in context of culture
- Affinity and team dynamics in technical organizations
- Measuring and sustaining DevOps transformation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're implementing or scaling DevOps and want to focus on the cultural and organizational dimensions beyond just tooling.
