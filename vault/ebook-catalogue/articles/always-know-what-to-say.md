# Always Know What to Say
**Author:** Peter W. Murphy

## What This Book Is About
A practical guide to overcoming social anxiety and mastering the art of conversation. Murphy provides easy-to-apply techniques for approaching and talking to anyone, covering how to start conversations, keep them going, and build rapport in both personal and professional settings.

## Key Topics
- Overcoming social anxiety and shyness
- Starting conversations with strangers
- Small talk techniques and conversation starters
- Building rapport and confidence
- Communication skills for networking

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want quick, actionable tips for becoming more confident and effective in social interactions.
