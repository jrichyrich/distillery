# From Strength to Strength
**Author:** Arthur C. Brooks

## What This Book Is About
Brooks addresses a crisis many high achievers face: the inevitable decline of the "fluid intelligence" that powered their early success. Drawing on research in psychology, neuroscience, and philosophy, he shows how to pivot from fluid to crystallized intelligence — shifting from innovation to wisdom, from accumulation to generosity — to find deeper happiness and purpose in the second half of life.

## Key Topics
- Fluid vs. crystallized intelligence across the lifespan
- The "striver's curse" and addiction to professional success
- Pivoting from achievement to meaning and purpose
- The role of spirituality and relationships in later-life happiness
- Practical strategies for a fulfilling second act

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're mid-career or beyond and wrestling with how to find renewed purpose as your early-career strengths naturally evolve.
