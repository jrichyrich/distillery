# Supercommunicators
**Author:** Charles Duhigg

## What This Book Is About
From the author of The Power of Habit, Supercommunicators explores how the best communicators unlock the secret language of connection. Duhigg reveals that every conversation operates on one of three levels -- practical, emotional, or social -- and that supercommunicators instinctively match the right level. The book combines research with real-world stories to show how anyone can become a more effective communicator.

## Key Topics
- The three types of conversations (practical, emotional, social)
- How to identify and match conversational wavelengths
- The neuroscience of human connection
- Active listening and empathy techniques
- Building deeper relationships through better dialogue

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to improve your ability to connect with others in both personal and professional conversations.
