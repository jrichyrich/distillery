# How to Decide: Simple Tools for Making Better Choices
**Author:** Annie Duke

## What This Book Is About
Former professional poker player Annie Duke provides a practical toolkit for making better decisions by separating the quality of a decision from the quality of its outcome. The book offers concrete exercises and frameworks for overcoming cognitive biases, thinking in probabilities, and building a decision-making process that consistently leads to better results over time.

## Key Topics
- Resulting: the trap of judging decisions by outcomes
- Thinking in probabilities and expected value
- Decision matrices and pre-commitment strategies
- Overcoming hindsight bias and motivated reasoning
- The role of uncertainty in every decision
- Practical exercises for improving decision quality

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want hands-on tools and exercises for systematically improving your decision-making process.
