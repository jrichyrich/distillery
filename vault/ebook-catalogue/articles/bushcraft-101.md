# Bushcraft 101: A Field Guide to the Art of Wilderness Survival
**Author:** Dave Canterbury

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive field guide to wilderness survival from Dave Canterbury, co-star of the TV show "Dual Survival." The book covers essential bushcraft skills drawn from the traditions of frontiersmen and woodsmen, providing practical knowledge for surviving and thriving in the outdoors using both modern and traditional techniques.

## Key Topics
- Wilderness survival fundamentals
- Fire-making and shelter construction
- Water procurement and purification
- Navigation and land travel
- Tool use and knife skills
- Trapping, foraging, and wild food

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're heading into the wilderness and want a reliable reference for essential survival and bushcraft skills.
