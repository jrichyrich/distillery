# Mental Models
**Author:** Shane Parrish and Rhiannon Beaubien

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive collection of mental models and cognitive frameworks compiled from Farnam Street, inspired by Charlie Munger's approach to multidisciplinary thinking. The guide covers approximately 100 models across general thinking, physics, biology, systems thinking, and numeracy, providing simplified explanations of how things work to help make better decisions.

## Key Topics
- Circle of competence and first principles thinking
- Inversion, second-order thinking, and thought experiments
- Survivorship bias, hindsight bias, and commitment/consistency
- Systems thinking and numeracy frameworks
- Multidisciplinary approaches to decision-making

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a quick reference guide to the most important mental models across disciplines.
