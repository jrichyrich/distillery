# God King
**Author:** Graham McNeill

## What This Book Is About
A Warhammer Fantasy novel telling the story of Sigmar, the legendary founder of the Empire, as he faces the greatest threat to his fledgling nation. Sigmar must unite the tribes of men against the forces of the undead led by the necromancer Nagash, in a war that will determine whether humanity survives or falls to darkness.

## Key Topics
- Sigmar's legendary battles to defend the Empire
- The threat of Nagash and the undead
- Uniting fractious human tribes against a common enemy
- Heroism, sacrifice, and the forging of a nation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want epic Warhammer Fantasy centered on Sigmar's legendary wars and the founding of the Empire.
