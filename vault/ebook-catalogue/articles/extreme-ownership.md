# Extreme Ownership: How U.S. Navy SEALs Lead and Win
**Author:** Jocko Willink, Leif Babin

## What This Book Is About
Drawing from their combat experiences leading SEAL Task Unit Bruiser in Ramadi, Iraq, Willink and Babin distill battlefield leadership lessons into principles applicable to any team or organization. The core thesis is that leaders must own everything in their world — there are no bad teams, only bad leaders — and that this radical accountability is the foundation of all high performance.

## Key Topics
- Extreme ownership and radical accountability
- Decentralized command and empowering subordinates
- Prioritize and execute under pressure
- Cover and move (teamwork across silos)
- Leading up and down the chain of command
- Simple, clear communication in high-stakes environments

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a framework for radical leadership accountability or want to apply military-grade leadership principles to business challenges.
