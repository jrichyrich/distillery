# CHFI Practice Questions
**Author:** Sam Ali

## What This Book Is About
A practice question bank for the Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator (CHFI) certification exam. The book covers forensic investigation procedures, from preparing computers for examination to securing evidence, identifying data types, and following proper chain-of-custody protocols.

## Key Topics
- Computer forensics investigation procedures
- Digital evidence collection and preservation
- CHFI certification exam preparation
- Incident response fundamentals
- Forensic tool usage and methodology
- Chain of custody and legal considerations

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're preparing for the CHFI certification exam and need practice questions to test your knowledge of computer forensics procedures.
