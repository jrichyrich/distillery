# The Truth in Small Doses
**Author:** Clifton Leaf

## What This Book Is About
The Truth in Small Doses is an investigative look at why the decades-long "war on cancer" has failed to deliver on its promises. Journalist Clifton Leaf, himself a cancer survivor, examines the systemic problems in cancer research -- from misaligned incentives and bureaucratic inertia to the focus on incremental treatments over prevention and cures. The book proposes a path forward for actually winning the fight against cancer.

## Key Topics
- Why progress against cancer has stalled
- Systemic failures in cancer research funding and incentives
- The gap between research spending and patient outcomes
- Prevention vs. treatment in the cancer paradigm
- Proposals for reforming the cancer research enterprise

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand why we are losing the war on cancer and what it would take to change course.
