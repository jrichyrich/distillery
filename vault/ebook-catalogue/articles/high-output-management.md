# High Output Management
**Author:** Andrew S. Grove

## What This Book Is About
Intel co-founder and CEO Andy Grove distills his management philosophy into a practical guide that treats management as a high-output production process. Grove covers everything from running effective meetings and making decisions to performance reviews and organizational structure, all viewed through the lens of maximizing a manager's output — defined as the output of their organization plus the output of neighboring organizations they influence.

## Key Topics
- Management as a production process (leverage, output)
- Running effective meetings and one-on-ones
- Performance reviews and task-relevant maturity
- Decision-making and planning in fast-moving organizations
- Organizational structure and dual reporting

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a timeless, no-nonsense operating manual for being an effective manager, especially in technology organizations.
