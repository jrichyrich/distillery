# Start with Why
**Author:** Simon Sinek

## What This Book Is About
Start with Why explores how great leaders and organizations inspire action by starting with their purpose -- their "why" -- rather than what they do or how they do it. Simon Sinek introduces the Golden Circle framework (Why, How, What) and uses examples from Apple, Martin Luther King Jr., and the Wright Brothers to show that people do not buy what you do, they buy why you do it. The book makes the case that purpose-driven leadership creates loyalty, innovation, and lasting success.

## Key Topics
- The Golden Circle: Why, How, What
- How great leaders inspire rather than manipulate
- The biology of trust and decision-making
- Building organizations around a clear purpose
- The tipping point of mass adoption
- Maintaining your why as you grow

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to articulate your organization's purpose or want to understand how the most inspiring leaders create movements rather than just products.
