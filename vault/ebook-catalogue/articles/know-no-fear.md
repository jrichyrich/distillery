# Know No Fear
**Author:** Dan Abnett

## What This Book Is About
A Horus Heresy novel set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, depicting the devastating Battle of Calth where the Word Bearers legion launches a surprise attack against the Ultramarines. Dan Abnett chronicles one of the most pivotal betrayals of the Heresy era in a visceral, real-time narrative style.

## Key Topics
- The Battle of Calth and the Ultramarines' betrayal
- The Horus Heresy and galactic civil war
- Military science fiction and tactical combat
- Loyalty, betrayal, and survival

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are reading the Horus Heresy series or want an intense military sci-fi battle narrative.
