# Toxic Productivity
**Author:** Israa Nasir

## What This Book Is About
Toxic Productivity challenges the relentless pursuit of doing more that defines hustle culture. Therapist Israa Nasir combines brain science, clinical research, and personal anecdotes to reveal the deep-seated beliefs driving overwork and the toll it takes on mental health. The book helps readers identify when productivity becomes toxic and offers actionable strategies to reclaim time, energy, and self-worth without sacrificing achievement.

## Key Topics
- How hustle culture creates toxic productivity
- The psychology behind compulsive overachievement
- Recognizing when productivity harms mental health
- Redefining self-worth beyond output
- Practical strategies for sustainable productivity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you suspect your drive to be productive has crossed the line into something that is hurting you.
