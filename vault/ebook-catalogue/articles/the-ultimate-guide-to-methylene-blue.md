# The Ultimate Guide to Methylene Blue
**Author:** Mark Sloan

## What This Book Is About
The Ultimate Guide to Methylene Blue explores the history, science, and potential therapeutic applications of methylene blue, one of the oldest synthetic compounds in medicine. Sloan covers its proposed benefits for conditions ranging from depression and cognitive decline to cancer and heart disease, all through the lens of mitochondrial dysfunction and metabolic medicine. The book presents methylene blue as a tool for targeting the root causes of chronic disease.

## Key Topics
- History and pharmacology of methylene blue
- Mitochondrial dysfunction and chronic disease
- Proposed cognitive and neuroprotective benefits
- Metabolic medicine as a paradigm shift
- Safety considerations and dosing information

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a deep dive into the science and therapeutic potential of methylene blue.
