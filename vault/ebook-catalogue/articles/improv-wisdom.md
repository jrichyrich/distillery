# Improv Wisdom: Don't Prepare, Just Show Up
**Author:** Patricia Ryan Madson

## What This Book Is About
A Stanford drama professor distills the core principles of improvisational theater into thirteen maxims for living a more spontaneous, creative, and fulfilling life. Madson shows how saying yes, embracing the unexpected, and letting go of perfectionism can transform everyday situations at work and at home.

## Key Topics
- Saying yes and embracing what happens
- Letting go of over-preparation and perfectionism
- Being present and attentive in the moment
- Applying improv principles to relationships and work
- Building confidence through spontaneity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel paralyzed by over-planning and want practical wisdom on being more present and adaptable.
