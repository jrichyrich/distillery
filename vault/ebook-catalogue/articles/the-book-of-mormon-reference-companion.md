# The Book of Mormon Reference Companion
**Author:** Dennis L. Largey

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive alphabetical encyclopedia covering people, places, doctrines, and themes found in the Book of Mormon. Published by Deseret Book, it provides scholarly entries from multiple contributors on everything from individual characters (Aaron, Abinadi, Alma) to theological concepts (Abrahamic covenant, accountability, agency) with an overview of the book's structure and coming forth.

## Key Topics
- Alphabetical entries on Book of Mormon people and places
- Doctrinal and theological analysis
- Historical and textual overview of the Book of Mormon
- Contributions from multiple LDS scholars
- Cross-references and study aids

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need a quick, authoritative reference on any person, place, or doctrine mentioned in the Book of Mormon.
