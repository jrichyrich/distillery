# Follow the Science
**Author:** Sharyl Attkisson

## What This Book Is About
Investigative journalist Sharyl Attkisson examines how the phrase "follow the science" has been weaponized to shut down debate, manipulate public opinion, and advance agendas that often have little to do with actual science. The book explores how media, government, and corporate interests have distorted scientific discourse on topics from public health to environmental policy.

## Key Topics
- How "the science" gets distorted by media and politics
- Case studies of scientific narratives that misled the public
- The role of corporate and government influence on research
- Critical thinking about scientific claims in the media
- Investigative journalism techniques for evaluating evidence

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a skeptical, investigative look at how scientific authority is used and abused in public discourse.
