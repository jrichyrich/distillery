# It IS About Islam
**Author:** Glenn Beck

## What This Book Is About
Glenn Beck examines the ideological roots of radical Islamic terrorism, focusing on ISIS, Al Qaeda, and the concept of the caliphate. The book is part of Beck's Control Series and presents his argument that understanding the religious motivations behind extremism is essential to combating it.

## Key Topics
- The ideology behind ISIS and radical Islamic movements
- Historical roots of the caliphate concept
- Distinguishing radical ideology from mainstream Islam
- National security implications
- Media and political narratives around terrorism

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a conservative perspective on the ideological drivers behind Islamic extremism.
