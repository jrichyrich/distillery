# First and Only
**Author:** Dan Abnett

## What This Book Is About
The first novel in Dan Abnett's celebrated Gaunt's Ghosts series, following Colonel-Commissar Ibram Gaunt and his regiment of light infantry — the Tanith First and Only — as they fight across the battlefields of the Sabbat Worlds Crusade. Part military fiction, part mystery, the story weaves battlefield action with a conspiracy surrounding a powerful Chaos artifact.

## Key Topics
- Military life in the Warhammer 40K Imperial Guard
- Leadership and loyalty under impossible conditions
- The Sabbat Worlds Crusade campaign
- Regimental politics and inter-unit rivalry

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want gritty, ground-level military science fiction set in the Warhammer 40K universe with compelling characters and sharp action.
