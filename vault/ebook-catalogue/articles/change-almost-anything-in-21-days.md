# Change Almost Anything in 21 Days
**Author:** Ruth Fishel

## What This Book Is About
A guide to using the power of affirmations to transform habits and mindset in 21 days. Fishel provides over 500 affirmations along with a structured method for rewriting self-talk, overcoming fear, and removing barriers to change. The book includes real-life success stories covering weight loss, financial goals, health recovery, and building self-confidence.

## Key Topics
- The science and practice of affirmations
- Overcoming negative self-talk and limiting beliefs
- 21-day habit change methodology
- Fear, courage, and removing barriers to change
- Mindfulness and present-moment awareness
- Real-life transformation stories

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a structured, affirmation-based approach to changing a specific habit or mindset pattern.
