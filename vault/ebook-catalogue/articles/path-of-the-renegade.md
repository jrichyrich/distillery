# Path of the Renegade
**Author:** Andy Chambers

## What This Book Is About
Path of the Renegade is a Warhammer 40,000 novel set in the twisted dark city of Commorragh, home of the Dark Eldar (Drukhari). It follows the machinations of a disgraced archon seeking to reclaim power through treachery, alliance, and forbidden knowledge. The novel is the first book in the Dark Eldar Path trilogy, exploring the decadent and brutal society of the Drukhari.

## Key Topics
- Dark Eldar political intrigue and power struggles
- The labyrinthine city of Commorragh
- Drukhari culture, castes, and hierarchy
- Grimdark science fiction warfare and survival

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a deep dive into the darkest corners of the Warhammer 40,000 universe through a story of betrayal, ambition, and alien cruelty.
