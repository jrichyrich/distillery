# Navigating the Cybersecurity Career Path
**Author:** Helen E. Patton

## What This Book Is About
This book provides practical guidance for anyone looking to enter or advance in the cybersecurity profession. Helen Patton, a seasoned CISO, covers the many career paths available within cybersecurity, from technical roles to leadership positions. The book addresses how to build skills, gain experience, and navigate the unique challenges of a cybersecurity career at every stage.

## Key Topics
- Cybersecurity career paths and specializations
- Building technical and soft skills for infosec
- Transitioning into cybersecurity from other fields
- Advancing from practitioner to leadership roles
- Navigating organizational dynamics as a security professional
- Certifications, education, and continuous learning

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are planning your cybersecurity career trajectory or helping someone else chart their path into the security profession.
