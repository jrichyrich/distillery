# The Attributes
**Author:** Rich Diviney

## What This Book Is About
A former Navy SEAL commander reveals the 25 hidden attributes that drive optimal performance. Drawing from his experience selecting candidates for SEAL teams, Diviney argues that skills matter less than underlying attributes like grit, adaptability, patience, and situational awareness. He provides a framework for understanding and developing these deeper drivers of success.

## Key Topics
- 25 attributes of optimal performance
- Navy SEAL selection and what predicts success
- Temperament vs. skills in high-performance environments
- Resilience, adaptability, and mental toughness
- Leadership under extreme conditions
- Self-assessment of personal attributes

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand what truly drives performance beyond skills and credentials, or when building high-performing teams.
