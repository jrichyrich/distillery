# 14 Days to Fitness Freedom: The Garage Gym
**Author:** Unknown Author

## What This Book Is About
A practical 14-day guide to building a fully operational garage gym from scratch. The author shares their personal experience starting with minimal equipment and walks readers through daily steps including purchasing key items, building DIY equipment like parallettes, plyometric boxes, and power racks, and completing their first workout.

## Key Topics
- Setting up a home garage gym on a budget
- DIY fitness equipment construction (parallettes, plyo box, power rack, rings)
- Flooring and gym layout planning
- Beginner workout programming
- Equipment selection and prioritization

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a quick, step-by-step blueprint for building a functional home gym in your garage without breaking the bank.
