# Ideaflow
**Author:** Jeremy Utley and Perry Klebahn

## What This Book Is About
Two Stanford d.school professors share their framework for turning creativity into a reliable, daily practice. The book argues that the volume of ideas (ideaflow) is the key metric for innovation and provides tools and techniques for generating more and better ideas consistently, whether you are a founder, leader, or aspiring innovator.

## Key Topics
- Ideaflow as a measurable metric for creative output
- Tools and exercises for generating more ideas
- Building a founder's mindset before the product is ready
- Establishing creativity as a daily practice
- Lessons from Stanford's design thinking program

## When To Reach For This Book
When you or your team feel stuck creatively and need a systematic approach to generating better ideas.
