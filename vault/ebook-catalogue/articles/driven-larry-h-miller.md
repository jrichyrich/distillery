# Driven: An Autobiography
**Author:** Larry H. Miller with Doug Robinson

## What This Book Is About
The autobiography of Larry H. Miller, the legendary Utah businessman who built a billion-dollar empire of car dealerships, owned the Utah Jazz NBA franchise, and became one of the most influential figures in the American West. Miller chronicles his rise from a parts counter worker to a business titan, revealing the relentless drive, deal-making instincts, and personal struggles behind his success.

## Key Topics
- Building a business empire from nothing
- Ownership and leadership of the Utah Jazz
- Entrepreneurial grit and relentless work ethic
- Balancing ambition with family and health
- Lessons from failure and perseverance

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want an inspiring story of entrepreneurial determination and the real costs and rewards of building something massive.
