# The First Heretic
**Author:** Aaron Dembski-Bowden

## What This Book Is About
Book 14 in the Horus Heresy series, this novel tells the story of the Word Bearers Legion — the first Space Marines to fall to Chaos. After being publicly humiliated by the Emperor for their religious worship, the Word Bearers and their Primarch Lorgar embark on a secret pilgrimage that leads them into the arms of the Chaos Gods, setting the stage for the galaxy-spanning civil war known as the Horus Heresy.

## Key Topics
- The fall of the Word Bearers to Chaos worship
- Lorgar's crisis of faith and search for divinity
- The origins of the Horus Heresy rebellion
- The tension between faith, loyalty, and obedience

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're reading through the Horus Heresy series or want one of the best-written explorations of how faith can be corrupted in the Warhammer 40K universe.
