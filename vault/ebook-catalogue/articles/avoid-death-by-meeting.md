# Avoid Death by Meeting
**Author:** Patrick Lencioni

## What This Book Is About
Lencioni argues that meetings fail for two reasons: they lack drama (making them boring) and they lack context and purpose (mixing administrivia, tactics, and strategy into unfocused sessions). Drawing lessons from Hollywood screenwriting, he provides a framework for making meetings more engaging by nurturing productive conflict and structuring different meeting types for different purposes.

## Key Topics
- Why meetings are ineffective (lack of drama and context)
- Using conflict to drive meeting engagement
- Structuring meetings by type and purpose
- Hollywood storytelling principles applied to meetings
- Leadership responsibility for meeting quality

## When To Reach For This Book
When your meetings feel unproductive and you need a quick, actionable framework for transforming how your team meets.
