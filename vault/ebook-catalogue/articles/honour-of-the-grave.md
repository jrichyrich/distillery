# Honour of the Grave
**Author:** Robin D. Laws

## What This Book Is About
A Warhammer Fantasy novel set in the Old World of the Empire, where mighty heroes battle against the forces of Chaos, the undead, and the many horrors that threaten civilization. In a dark age of daemons and sorcery, the story follows adventures through the Empire's great mountains, dark forests, and vast cities as threats close in from every direction.

## Key Topics
- Adventure in the Warhammer Fantasy Old World
- The Empire and its defenders against darkness
- Undead and Chaos threats to civilization
- Dark fantasy action and intrigue

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a classic Warhammer Fantasy adventure set in the grim and perilous Old World.
