# The Culture Code
**Author:** Daniel Coyle

## What This Book Is About
The Culture Code reveals the secrets of highly successful groups by examining the cultures of organizations like Google, Pixar, the Navy SEALs, and the San Antonio Spurs. Daniel Coyle identifies three core skills that drive group culture: building safety, sharing vulnerability, and establishing purpose. The book provides actionable insights for anyone looking to build a stronger, more cohesive team or organization.

## Key Topics
- Building psychological safety within groups
- The power of vulnerability in team dynamics
- Creating shared purpose and clear signals
- What the best teams do differently
- Practical culture-building techniques

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand and improve the culture of your team or organization.
