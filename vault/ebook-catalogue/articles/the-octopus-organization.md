# The Octopus Organization
**Author:** Phil Le-Brun and Jana Werner

## What This Book Is About
A guide to building organizations that can thrive amid constant change, using the octopus as a metaphor for adaptive, distributed intelligence. Le-Brun and Werner present frameworks for creating organizations that sense, respond, and transform continuously rather than relying on periodic restructuring or top-down mandates.

## Key Topics
- Adaptive organizational design
- Distributed decision-making and autonomy
- Continuous transformation versus periodic change
- Building organizational resilience and agility
- Leading through complexity and uncertainty

## When To Reach For This Book
When your organization needs to become more adaptive and you want a fresh framework for continuous transformation.
