# Drawing on the Right Side of the Brain
**Author:** Betty Edwards

## What This Book Is About
The definitive 4th edition of the classic art instruction book that teaches anyone to draw by shifting from left-brain analytical processing to right-brain visual/perceptual mode. Edwards provides exercises that train you to see like an artist — perceiving edges, spaces, relationships, and light — proving that drawing is a learnable skill, not an innate talent.

## Key Topics
- Left-brain vs. right-brain modes of perception
- Contour drawing and perceiving edges
- Negative space and spatial relationships
- Light, shadow, and tonal values
- Overcoming symbolic thinking that blocks realistic drawing

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to learn to draw or improve your visual perception skills through a proven, structured approach.
