# The Easy Way to Quit Caffeine
**Author:** Allen Carr

## What This Book Is About
Applying Allen Carr's famous Easyway method to caffeine addiction, this book reframes the quitting process so it feels like liberation rather than deprivation. Carr challenges the beliefs that keep people dependent on caffeine — that it provides energy, focus, or pleasure — and helps readers break free without willpower, withdrawal suffering, or feeling like they're missing out.

## Key Topics
- The Easyway method applied to caffeine dependency
- Debunking myths about caffeine's benefits
- Understanding the addiction cycle of caffeine
- Quitting without willpower or deprivation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to quit or reduce caffeine intake and prefer a psychological reframing approach over white-knuckling it.
