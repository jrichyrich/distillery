# The Ten Bushcraft Books
**Author:** Richard Graves

## What This Book Is About
The Ten Bushcraft Books is a classic collection of survival and wilderness skills originally written by Richard Graves, a Commanding Officer of the Australian Jungle Survival and Rescue Detachment. Originally developed as wartime rescue mission guides, the notes were later revised for a School of Bushcraft that ran for nearly 20 years. The text covers comprehensive outdoor survival techniques with original illustrations by the author.

## Key Topics
- Wilderness survival fundamentals
- Jungle survival and rescue techniques
- Fire-making, shelter, and water procurement
- Navigation and outdoor self-reliance
- Bushcraft skills for varied environments

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a comprehensive, classic reference on wilderness survival and bushcraft skills.
