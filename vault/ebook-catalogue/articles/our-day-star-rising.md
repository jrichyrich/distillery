# Our Day Star Rising
**Author:** Jeffrey R. Holland

## What This Book Is About
Elder Jeffrey R. Holland of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles offers a devotional exploration of the New Testament, bringing his characteristic eloquence and spiritual insight to the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. The book guides readers through the Gospels, Acts, and Epistles with personal commentary, doctrinal reflection, and inspiring application.

## Key Topics
- The life and ministry of Jesus Christ
- Apostolic commentary on the New Testament
- Doctrinal insights from a Latter-day Saint apostle
- Personal application of New Testament teachings
- The relevance of the New Testament today

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a spiritually rich, apostolic companion for your study of the New Testament.
