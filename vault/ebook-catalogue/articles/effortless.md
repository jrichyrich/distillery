# Effortless
**Author:** Greg McKeown

## What This Book Is About
The follow-up to Essentialism, this book tackles the question: once you've identified what matters most, how do you make doing those things as easy as possible? McKeown argues that the path to peak contribution isn't grinding harder but finding the effortless way — simplifying processes, achieving a state of ease, and building systems that produce results with less friction.

## Key Topics
- The Effortless State: how to be physically rested and emotionally unburdened
- The Effortless Action: how to make essential work easier
- The Effortless Result: how to achieve results that flow without constant effort
- Overcoming the assumption that important work must be hard

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're burned out from grinding on essential work and need strategies to make the important things easier, not just prioritized.
