# To All the Boys I've Loved Before
**Author:** Jenny Han

## What This Book Is About
To All the Boys I've Loved Before is a young adult romance about Lara Jean Covey, a teenage girl who writes secret love letters to every boy she has ever had a crush on -- letters she never intends to send. When the letters are mysteriously mailed out, Lara Jean's quiet life is turned upside down as she navigates the fallout, fake relationships, and real feelings. The novel spawned a bestselling trilogy and a popular Netflix film series.

## Key Topics
- First love and the vulnerability of crushes
- Family bonds and sisterhood
- Navigating high school social dynamics
- The gap between fantasy and real relationships
- Growing up and finding your own identity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a charming, heartfelt YA romance about love, family, and growing up.
