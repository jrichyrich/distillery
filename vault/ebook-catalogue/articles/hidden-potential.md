# Hidden Potential
**Author:** Adam Grant

## What This Book Is About
Adam Grant challenges the cult of natural talent, arguing that greatness is less about innate gifts and more about the character skills that help you grow — skills like the courage to embrace discomfort, the discipline to absorb information, and the capacity to see possibility where others see limitations. Drawing on research and stories from education, sports, and business, Grant reveals how anyone can build the skills to unlock their hidden potential.

## Key Topics
- Character skills vs. innate talent as drivers of achievement
- The science of learning and skill development
- Embracing discomfort as a growth mechanism
- Systems and scaffolding that help people reach their potential
- Rethinking how we identify and develop talent

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want evidence-based strategies for developing yourself or others beyond what raw talent alone would predict.
