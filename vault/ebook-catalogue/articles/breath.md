# Breath: The New Science of a Lost Art
**Author:** James Nestor

## What This Book Is About
James Nestor explores the science and history of human breathing, revealing how modern humans have lost the ability to breathe correctly and how this affects our health. Drawing on cutting-edge research, ancient practices, and self-experimentation, Nestor shows how simple changes in breathing patterns can improve athletic performance, rejuvenate organs, and halt disease progression.

## Key Topics
- The science of breathing and respiratory health
- Nasal breathing vs. mouth breathing
- Historical and cultural breathing practices
- Breathing techniques for health optimization
- The connection between breathing and chronic disease
- Freediving and extreme breathing research

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand how breathing affects your health and learn practical techniques for breathing better.
