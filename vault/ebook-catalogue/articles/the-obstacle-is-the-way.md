# The Obstacle Is the Way
**Author:** Ryan Holiday

## What This Book Is About
Drawing on the Stoic philosophy of Marcus Aurelius, Ryan Holiday argues that the impediment to action advances action -- that what stands in the way becomes the way. Through historical examples from Ulysses S. Grant to Steve Jobs, he presents a three-part framework of Perception, Action, and Will for turning obstacles into advantages.

## Key Topics
- Stoic philosophy applied to modern challenges
- The discipline of perception: seeing clearly under pressure
- The discipline of action: persistent, creative effort
- The discipline of will: endurance and acceptance
- Historical examples of turning adversity into advantage

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are facing a significant obstacle and need a philosophical framework for turning it into an opportunity.
