# Clear Thinking: Turning Ordinary Moments into Extraordinary Results
**Author:** Shane Parrish

## What This Book Is About
Shane Parrish, founder of Farnam Street, argues that the quality of our lives depends on the quality of our thinking in ordinary moments, not just the big decisions. The book provides frameworks for recognizing and overcoming the default patterns (emotion, ego, social, and inertia defaults) that hijack our thinking, and replacing them with clear, rational decision-making.

## Key Topics
- Cognitive defaults that sabotage clear thinking
- Decision-making frameworks and mental models
- Emotion, ego, social, and inertia defaults
- Turning ordinary moments into better outcomes
- Building judgment and wisdom over time
- Practical tools from Farnam Street methodology

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to improve your everyday decision-making by understanding and overcoming the automatic patterns that lead to poor choices.
