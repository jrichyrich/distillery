# Outlive
**Author:** Peter Attia, MD

## What This Book Is About
Outlive presents a science-based framework for extending not just lifespan but healthspan -- the period of life spent in good health. Dr. Peter Attia draws on cutting-edge research to challenge conventional medical thinking about the "Four Horsemen" of chronic disease: heart disease, cancer, neurodegenerative disease, and type 2 diabetes. The book provides actionable strategies across exercise, nutrition, sleep, and emotional health to help readers live longer and better.

## Key Topics
- The concept of Medicine 3.0 and proactive health strategy
- The Centenarian Decathlon framework for physical fitness goals
- Metabolic health and insulin resistance
- Cancer prevention and early detection strategies
- Cardiovascular disease risk reduction
- Neurodegenerative disease prevention
- Exercise as the most powerful longevity drug
- Nutritional biochemistry and personalized nutrition
- Sleep optimization and emotional health

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a comprehensive, evidence-based guide to taking control of your long-term health and designing a personalized longevity strategy.
