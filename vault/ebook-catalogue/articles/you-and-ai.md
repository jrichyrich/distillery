# You and AI
**Author:** Rajeev Ronanki

## What This Book Is About
You and AI is a citizen's guide to understanding how artificial intelligence and blockchain are reshaping the future of healthcare. Ronanki, a healthcare technology executive, explains in conversational style how these technologies can improve health outcomes, transform patient experiences, and reshape our relationship with healthcare organizations. The book addresses both the promise and the responsibilities that come with AI-driven healthcare.

## Key Topics
- AI applications in healthcare and medicine
- Blockchain's role in health data and trust
- Building AI-first healthcare organizations
- Citizen rights and responsibilities in an AI world
- The practical path from innovation to business success

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand how AI and blockchain are transforming healthcare from a practical, human-centered perspective.
