# Experiential Intelligence
**Author:** Soren Kaplan

## What This Book Is About
Kaplan introduces the concept of Experiential Intelligence (XQ) — the mindsets, abilities, and know-how gained from your unique life experiences — as a complement to IQ and EQ. The book provides a framework for leaders and organizations to recognize, assess, and leverage the hidden strengths that come from diverse lived experiences, driving transformation and building more inclusive, capable teams.

## Key Topics
- Experiential Intelligence (XQ) as a complement to IQ and EQ
- How life experiences shape leadership capabilities
- Unlocking hidden strengths in yourself and your teams
- Building diverse, experience-rich organizations
- Frameworks for assessing and developing XQ

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand how lived experiences create unique leadership capabilities and how to harness diverse experience for organizational advantage.
