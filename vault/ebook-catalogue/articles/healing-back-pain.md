# Healing Back Pain: The Mind-Body Connection
**Author:** John E. Sarno

## What This Book Is About
Dr. Sarno presents his groundbreaking theory that most chronic back pain is not caused by structural abnormalities but by Tension Myositis Syndrome (TMS) — a condition where repressed emotions, particularly rage, manifest as physical pain. The book explains how the brain creates pain as a distraction from uncomfortable emotions, and how simply understanding this mechanism can eliminate the pain without surgery, drugs, or physical therapy.

## Key Topics
- Tension Myositis Syndrome (TMS) theory
- The mind-body connection in chronic pain
- How repressed emotions manifest as physical symptoms
- Why structural diagnoses (herniated discs, etc.) are often misleading
- Knowledge therapy as treatment for chronic back pain

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're dealing with chronic back pain that hasn't responded to conventional treatments and want to explore the mind-body connection.
