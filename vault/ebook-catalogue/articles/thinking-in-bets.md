# Thinking in Bets
**Author:** Annie Duke

## What This Book Is About
Thinking in Bets draws on Annie Duke's career as a professional poker player to reframe how we make decisions under uncertainty. Duke argues that life is more like poker than chess -- full of hidden information and luck -- and that thinking in terms of bets helps us embrace uncertainty, avoid resulting (judging decisions by outcomes), and separate skill from luck. The book provides a practical framework for making smarter decisions in any domain.

## Key Topics
- Thinking probabilistically about decisions
- Separating decision quality from outcome quality
- Overcoming resulting and hindsight bias
- Building a truthseeking group for accountability
- Embracing uncertainty as a decision-making advantage

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a practical framework for making better decisions under uncertainty by thinking like a poker player.
