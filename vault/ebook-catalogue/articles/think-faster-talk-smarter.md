# Think Faster, Talk Smarter
**Author:** Matt Abrahams

## What This Book Is About
Think Faster, Talk Smarter provides a six-step approach to mastering spontaneous communication -- the unplanned moments when you are put on the spot. Stanford communication lecturer Matt Abrahams shares actionable techniques for managing anxiety, thinking on your feet, and speaking confidently in impromptu situations, from Q&A sessions to elevator pitches to difficult conversations. The book emphasizes connection over perfection.

## Key Topics
- A six-step framework for spontaneous speaking
- Managing speaking anxiety in the moment
- Structuring impromptu responses effectively
- Connection over perfection as a mindset
- Techniques drawn from improv and behavioral science

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to stop freezing up and start communicating with confidence whenever you are put on the spot.
