# Insurgent
**Author:** Veronica Roth

## What This Book Is About
The second book in the Divergent trilogy, Insurgent follows Tris Prior as she navigates an increasingly fractured dystopian society on the brink of all-out war between factions. Tris must confront grief, betrayal, and difficult moral choices as she uncovers secrets that could reshape her world.

## Key Topics
- Faction conflict and societal collapse
- Identity, loyalty, and moral ambiguity
- Grief, loss, and personal transformation
- Power, rebellion, and political intrigue

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are reading the Divergent series and want a fast-paced dystopian sequel full of twists.
