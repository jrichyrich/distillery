# Revelations in Context
**Author:** Matthew McBride & James Goldberg (Editors)

## What This Book Is About
Revelations in Context provides the historical stories behind the sections of the Doctrine and Covenants, a volume of scripture in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Drawing on insights from the Joseph Smith Papers project, the book places each revelation in its historical setting, helping readers understand the circumstances, questions, and challenges that prompted the revelations received by Joseph Smith and other early church leaders.

## Key Topics
- Historical context for Doctrine and Covenants sections
- Insights from the Joseph Smith Papers project
- Stories of early Latter-day Saint leaders and members
- The process of receiving and recording revelations
- The Restoration of the gospel in its historical setting

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to deepen your study of the Doctrine and Covenants by understanding the real-world circumstances behind each revelation.
