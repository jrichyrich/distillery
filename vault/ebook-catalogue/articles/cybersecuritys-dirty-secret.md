# Cybersecurity's Dirty Secret: Why Most Budgets Go to Waste
**Author:** Ross Young

## What This Book Is About
Ross Young exposes why most cybersecurity budgets fail to deliver meaningful security improvements. The book examines how organizations waste security spending on ineffective tools and approaches, and provides guidance for CISOs and executives on how to allocate security budgets more effectively to achieve real risk reduction.

## Key Topics
- Why cybersecurity budgets are wasted
- Common security spending mistakes
- Effective security budget allocation
- Measuring security ROI
- CISO strategy and executive communication
- Aligning spending with actual risk reduction

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to justify, optimize, or restructure your organization's cybersecurity budget to get better results from security spending.
