# Leadership Strategy and Tactics
**Author:** Jocko Willink

## What This Book Is About
Former Navy SEAL commander Jocko Willink provides a field manual for leadership at every level. Building on the principles of Extreme Ownership, this book offers granular, tactical advice for the everyday challenges leaders face -- from how to give direction without micromanaging, to handling underperformers, to earning trust in a new role.

## Key Topics
- Tactical leadership for everyday situations
- Balancing authority with humility
- Building and leading high-performance teams
- Handling difficult conversations and underperformers
- Applying military leadership principles to business

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need specific, actionable leadership tactics for real situations rather than abstract leadership theory.
