# A More Beautiful Question
**Author:** Warren Berger

## What This Book Is About
Journalist and innovation expert Warren Berger explores the power of questioning as the driving force behind innovation, creativity, and problem-solving. Through stories of entrepreneurs, designers, and change-makers, he shows how asking ambitious "Why?", "What if?", and "How?" questions leads to breakthrough ideas and demonstrates practical techniques for becoming a better questioner.

## Key Topics
- The Why / What If / How framework for innovation
- How questioning drives startups and creative breakthroughs
- Why we stop asking questions and how to start again
- The relationship between curiosity and innovation
- Practical techniques for asking better questions

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to sharpen your ability to ask the kind of questions that lead to real breakthroughs.
