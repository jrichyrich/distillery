# Social Engineering: The Art of Human Hacking
**Author:** Christopher Hadnagy

## What This Book Is About
Social Engineering: The Art of Human Hacking is a comprehensive guide to the techniques used by social engineers to manipulate people into divulging information or performing actions that compromise security. Christopher Hadnagy covers the full social engineering toolkit -- from pretexting and elicitation to phishing and physical infiltration -- while also explaining the psychological principles that make these attacks effective. The book serves both as a defensive resource and as a penetration testing reference.

## Key Topics
- The social engineering framework and attack lifecycle
- Pretexting, elicitation, and influence techniques
- Phishing and vishing attack methods
- Psychological principles of manipulation and persuasion
- Physical security and social engineering
- Building defenses against social engineering attacks

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to understand, test for, or defend against social engineering attacks targeting your organization's people.
