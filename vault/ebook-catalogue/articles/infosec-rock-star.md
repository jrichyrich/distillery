# Infosec Rock Star
**Author:** Ted Demopoulos

## What This Book Is About
A career development guide for information security professionals that goes beyond technical skills. Demopoulos shares practical advice on the methods, tools, and personal development techniques that set top infosec professionals apart, covering communication, networking, personal branding, and how to stand out in a crowd of talented technical people.

## Key Topics
- Career acceleration for security professionals
- Soft skills and personal development in infosec
- Building a professional reputation and personal brand
- Communication and leadership for technical people
- Balancing technical depth with business acumen

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are a technical security professional ready to level up your career beyond pure technical expertise.
