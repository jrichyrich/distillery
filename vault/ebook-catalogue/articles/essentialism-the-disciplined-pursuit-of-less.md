# Essentialism: The Disciplined Pursuit of Less
**Author:** Greg McKeown

## What This Book Is About
McKeown presents a systematic discipline for discerning what is absolutely essential and eliminating everything that is not, so you can make the highest possible contribution. Rather than trying to do everything, Essentialists invest their time and energy in the few things that truly matter — saying no to the trivial many so they can say yes to the vital few.

## Key Topics
- The Essentialist mindset vs. the non-essentialist trap
- Discerning the vital few from the trivial many
- The power of saying no and setting boundaries
- Creating space to explore, think, and play
- Executing effortlessly by removing obstacles

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're spread too thin, saying yes to everything, and need a framework for ruthless prioritization.
