# Tiny Habits
**Author:** BJ Fogg

## What This Book Is About
Tiny Habits presents Stanford behavior scientist BJ Fogg's breakthrough method for building lasting habits by starting incredibly small. Fogg's system is built on three elements: an anchor moment (an existing routine), a tiny behavior (the smallest version of the new habit), and instant celebration to wire in the behavior. The book argues that motivation is unreliable and that making habits tiny is the key to lasting change.

## Key Topics
- The Fogg Behavior Model (motivation, ability, prompt)
- Anchoring new habits to existing routines
- Why starting tiny beats relying on motivation
- The power of celebration in habit formation
- Designing behavior change systematically

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a proven, science-based method for building new habits that actually stick.
