# Why We Get Sick
**Author:** Benjamin Bikman

## What This Book Is About
Why We Get Sick argues that insulin resistance is the root cause of most modern chronic diseases, from heart disease and diabetes to Alzheimer's and cancer. Dr. Benjamin Bikman, a metabolic researcher, explains how elevated insulin levels damage nearly every system in the body and provides a concise roadmap for regaining metabolic health. The book bridges rigorous science with practical, accessible advice for the general reader.

## Key Topics
- Insulin resistance as the common driver of chronic disease
- How insulin affects the heart, brain, and reproductive system
- The role of diet and lifestyle in metabolic health
- Why standard dietary guidelines may worsen the problem
- Practical steps to improve insulin sensitivity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand insulin resistance and how to address the metabolic root cause of modern chronic disease.
