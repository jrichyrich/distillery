# The Flight of the Eisenstein
**Author:** James Swallow

## What This Book Is About
Book 4 in the Horus Heresy series, this novel follows Captain Nathaniel Garro of the Death Guard Legion as he witnesses his own Legion's corruption and makes a desperate flight aboard the frigate Eisenstein to warn the Emperor of Horus's betrayal. It is a story of loyalty, duty, and the agonizing choice to stand against your own brothers for the sake of what is right.

## Key Topics
- Loyalty vs. brotherhood in a time of betrayal
- The Death Guard Legion's fall to Nurgle
- Garro's desperate flight to warn Terra
- The founding seeds of what becomes the Inquisition

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're following the Horus Heresy series or want a tense, loyalty-driven thriller about standing firm when everything around you is crumbling.
