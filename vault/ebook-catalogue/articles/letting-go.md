# Letting Go
**Author:** David R. Hawkins

## What This Book Is About
Psychiatrist and spiritual teacher David Hawkins presents a practical mechanism for releasing negative emotions and limiting beliefs through the technique of surrender. Drawing on decades of clinical practice and his map of consciousness, Hawkins shows how letting go of suppressed feelings can lead to greater health, happiness, success, and ultimately spiritual enlightenment.

## Key Topics
- The mechanism of surrender and letting go
- Levels of consciousness and emotional calibration
- Releasing fear, guilt, anger, and grief
- The relationship between emotions and physical health
- Practical techniques for emotional freedom

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel weighed down by negative emotions and want a direct, practical method for releasing them.
