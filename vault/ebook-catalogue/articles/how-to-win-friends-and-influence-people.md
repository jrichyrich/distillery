# How to Win Friends and Influence People
**Author:** Dale Carnegie

## What This Book Is About
The timeless classic on interpersonal relationships, first published in 1936 and still one of the best-selling self-help books of all time. Carnegie lays out fundamental principles for handling people, making them like you, winning them to your way of thinking, and leading without arousing resentment — all grounded in genuine interest in others, empathy, and respect.

## Key Topics
- Fundamental techniques for handling people
- Six ways to make people like you
- How to win people to your way of thinking
- Being a leader and changing people without giving offense
- The power of genuine appreciation and listening

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to improve any relationship — professional or personal — through better understanding of human nature and communication.
