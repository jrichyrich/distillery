# The Science of Scaling
**Author:** Benjamin Hardy & Blake Erickson

## What This Book Is About
The Science of Scaling presents a paradigm-shifting framework for rapidly growing a business by redefining how leaders think about growth, culture, and strategy. Hardy and Erickson argue that scaling is fundamentally a leadership imperative: your organizational "floor" becomes your culture, and clarity becomes your competitive edge. The book provides practical strategies applicable to both startups and mature companies seeking breakthrough performance.

## Key Topics
- A new paradigm for business scaling and growth
- Leadership as the primary driver of scaling
- Building culture that raises the organizational floor
- Strategic clarity as a competitive advantage
- Using impossible goals as strategic filters
- Frameworks for achieving seemingly impossible growth goals
- Lessons from high-growth companies and leaders

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are a founder or leader looking to scale your organization and need a framework that integrates leadership, culture, and strategy.
