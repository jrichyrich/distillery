# Propaganda Wars
**Author:** Glenn Beck & Justin Haskins

## What This Book Is About
Propaganda Wars examines how global elites and institutions use propaganda to control public perception, thought, and emotion. Glenn Beck and Justin Haskins analyze the mechanisms of modern information manipulation across media, technology, and politics, arguing that citizens must recognize these tactics to protect their autonomy and freedom of thought.

## Key Topics
- Modern propaganda techniques and their historical roots
- Media manipulation and narrative control
- The role of technology and social media in shaping opinion
- Global power structures and information warfare
- Strategies for recognizing and resisting propaganda

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a conservative perspective on how modern information systems are used to shape public opinion and what individuals can do about it.
