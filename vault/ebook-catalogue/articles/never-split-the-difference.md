# Never Split the Difference
**Author:** Chris Voss and Tahl Raz

## What This Book Is About
Former FBI lead international kidnapping negotiator Chris Voss shares the field-tested techniques he developed during high-stakes hostage negotiations and shows how to apply them to business and personal life. The book introduces tactical empathy, calibrated questions, mirroring, labeling, and other counterintuitive strategies that challenge conventional negotiation wisdom.

## Key Topics
- Tactical empathy and emotional intelligence in negotiation
- Calibrated questions and the power of "no"
- Mirroring, labeling, and accusation audits
- The "black swan" theory of leverage
- High-stakes negotiation adapted for everyday situations

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to negotiate anything and want battle-tested techniques that go beyond "win-win" cliches.
