# Shadow King
**Author:** Gav Thorpe

## What This Book Is About
Shadow King is a Warhammer Fantasy novel that follows Alith Anar, the Shadow King, as he wages a guerrilla war against the Dark Elves led by the Witch King Malekith. Set during the Sundering -- the cataclysmic civil war that tore the elven civilization apart -- the novel chronicles Alith Anar's transformation from a noble prince into a ruthless shadow warrior bent on vengeance against those who destroyed his homeland of Nagarythe.

## Key Topics
- The Sundering and the elven civil war
- Alith Anar's transformation into the Shadow King
- Guerrilla warfare and resistance against tyranny
- The tragedy and corruption of the Dark Elves
- Themes of vengeance, loss, and endurance

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a dark, character-driven Warhammer Fantasy novel about one of the most iconic and tragic figures in elven lore.
