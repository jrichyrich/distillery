# Navigating the Digital Age: The Definitive Cybersecurity Guide for Directors and Officers
**Author:** Matt Rosenquist (Editor)

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive guide published by SecurityRoundtable.org aimed at helping corporate directors and officers understand and govern cybersecurity risk. The book covers the legal, regulatory, and strategic aspects of cybersecurity governance, providing practical guidance for board-level decision-making in the digital age.

## Key Topics
- Cybersecurity governance for boards and executives
- Legal and regulatory compliance frameworks
- Enterprise risk management for cyber threats
- Strategic oversight of information security programs
- Director and officer responsibilities in cyber incidents

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to understand or explain cybersecurity governance responsibilities at the board and executive level.
