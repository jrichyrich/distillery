# Nightbringer
**Author:** Graham McNeill

## What This Book Is About
The first novel in the Ultramarines series set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, following Captain Uriel Ventris as he leads his Space Marines into battle against a terrifying ancient threat. McNeill delivers a classic military science fiction story steeped in the grim darkness of the far future.

## Key Topics
- Captain Uriel Ventris and the Ultramarines chapter
- Ancient threats and xenos enemies
- Military tactics and Space Marine warfare
- Duty, honor, and sacrifice in the 41st millennium

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to start the Ultramarines series or enjoy Warhammer 40K military sci-fi.
