# Legion
**Author:** Dan Abnett

## What This Book Is About
The seventh book in the Horus Heresy series, Legion follows the mysterious Alpha Legion as they become entangled in an alien conspiracy that could determine the fate of the entire galaxy. Abnett weaves espionage, deception, and military action into a story that challenges assumptions about loyalty and the nature of the Heresy itself.

## Key Topics
- The enigmatic Alpha Legion and their methods
- Espionage, infiltration, and deception
- The Cabal and alien prophecy
- The Horus Heresy's broader galactic stakes
- Moral ambiguity and competing loyalties

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are reading the Horus Heresy series and want a spy-thriller take on the Warhammer 40K universe.
