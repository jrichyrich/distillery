# Blind Spots
**Author:** Marty Makary

## What This Book Is About
Johns Hopkins surgeon and public health researcher Marty Makary exposes the pitfalls of modern medicine, from entrenched dogma to misaligned incentives. Endorsed by Peter Attia and described as a "wake-up call," the book reveals how groupthink and institutional blind spots in healthcare lead to ineffective treatments and unnecessary harm, while pointing toward a more compassionate and evidence-based approach.

## Key Topics
- Blind spots in modern medical practice
- Medical dogma vs. evidence-based care
- Healthcare system dysfunction and reform
- New ideas disrupting traditional medicine
- Practical health education for patients

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand the hidden failures of modern medicine and become a more informed patient or healthcare leader.
