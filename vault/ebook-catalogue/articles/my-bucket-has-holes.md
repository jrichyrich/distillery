# My Bucket Has Holes
**Author:** Sarah Loucks

## What This Book Is About
A raw, personal memoir about living with Bipolar II disorder. Using the metaphor of a bucket with holes that cannot hold water the way others' can, Loucks chronicles her journey from childhood therapy through diagnosis, medication, and the daily struggle of managing a condition that is often invisible to others. The book is both a personal story and a window into the reality of bipolar disorder.

## Key Topics
- Living with Bipolar II disorder
- Childhood mental health and early therapy
- The experience of diagnosis and medication
- Shame, resilience, and self-acceptance
- Mental health awareness through personal narrative

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want an honest, first-person account of what it is like to live with Bipolar II disorder.
