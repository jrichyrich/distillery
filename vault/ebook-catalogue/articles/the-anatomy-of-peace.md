# The Anatomy of Peace
**Author:** The Arbinger Institute

## What This Book Is About
The Anatomy of Peace, from the Arbinger Institute, explores how our inner mindset -- whether we have a "heart at peace" or a "heart at war" -- shapes every conflict we experience. Through a narrative involving parents of troubled teens at a treatment program, the book reveals that resolving conflict requires not just different actions but a fundamentally different way of being. It builds on the ideas from Leadership and Self-Deception to offer practical strategies for genuine peacemaking.

## Key Topics
- Heart at peace vs. heart at war
- How self-betrayal fuels conflict
- Seeing others as people rather than objects
- Resolving deep interpersonal and family conflicts
- Practical steps toward genuine peacemaking

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are stuck in recurring conflicts and want to understand how your own mindset may be perpetuating them.
