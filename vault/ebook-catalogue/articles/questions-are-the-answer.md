# Questions Are the Answer
**Author:** Hal B. Gregersen

## What This Book Is About
Questions Are the Answer argues that the most important skill for driving innovation is not finding answers but asking better questions. Hal Gregersen, executive director of the MIT Leadership Center, draws on research with thousands of creative leaders to show how "catalytic questions" unlock breakthrough ideas. The book provides practical methods for cultivating a questioning mindset in yourself and your organization.

## Key Topics
- The power of catalytic questions in driving innovation
- Why most organizations suppress questioning
- Techniques for generating better questions
- Creating conditions that encourage questioning cultures
- Stories from innovative leaders across industries
- The Question Burst method for rapid ideation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel stuck on a problem and need to shift from seeking answers to asking fundamentally better questions.
