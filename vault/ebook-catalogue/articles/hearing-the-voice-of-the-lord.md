# Hearing the Voice of the Lord
**Author:** Gerald N. Lund

## What This Book Is About
Published by Deseret Book, this work by bestselling Latter-day Saint author Gerald Lund explores how God communicates with His children through the Holy Spirit. Lund examines the many ways personal revelation comes — through feelings, thoughts, dreams, scripture, and events — and provides practical guidance for recognizing, receiving, and acting on spiritual promptings.

## Key Topics
- Recognizing the voice of the Spirit
- Different forms and patterns of personal revelation
- Developing spiritual sensitivity and receptivity
- Distinguishing divine guidance from personal thoughts
- Scriptural examples of revelation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to deepen your ability to recognize and respond to spiritual promptings and personal revelation.
