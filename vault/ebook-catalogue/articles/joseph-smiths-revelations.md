# Joseph Smith's Revelations
**Author:** Matthew C. Godfrey, R. Eric Smith, Matthew J. Grow, and Ronald K. Esplin

## What This Book Is About
A study companion from the Joseph Smith Papers project that provides historical context, textual analysis, and scholarly commentary for the revelations found in the Doctrine and Covenants. This comprehensive resource helps readers understand the circumstances, meaning, and significance of each revelation in its original historical setting.

## Key Topics
- Historical context for each Doctrine and Covenants section
- Textual history and evolution of the revelations
- Insights from the Joseph Smith Papers project
- Key doctrinal themes in early Latter-day Saint revelation
- Scholarly methodology in studying religious texts

## When To Reach For This Book
When you are studying the Doctrine and Covenants and want deep historical and textual context from leading scholars.
