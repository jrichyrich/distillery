# A Thousand Sons
**Author:** Graham McNeill

## What This Book Is About
Book 12 in the Horus Heresy series, this novel tells the story of Magnus the Red and his Thousand Sons Legion — a chapter of scholar-warriors devoted to arcane knowledge and psychic power. When Magnus uses forbidden sorcery to warn the Emperor of Horus's treachery, he inadvertently destroys a vital Imperial project, bringing the wrath of the Space Wolves down upon his homeworld of Prospero in one of the most tragic events of the Heresy.

## Key Topics
- Magnus the Red and the Thousand Sons Legion
- The tension between knowledge and obedience
- The destruction of Prospero (the Burning of Prospero)
- Psychic power and the dangers of the Warp
- Tragedy of good intentions leading to catastrophe

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want one of the most tragic and philosophically rich entries in the Horus Heresy series.
