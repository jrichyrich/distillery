# Prospero Burns
**Author:** Dan Abnett

## What This Book Is About
Prospero Burns is the fifteenth novel in the Horus Heresy series set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. Told from the perspective of a human remembrancer embedded with the Space Wolves Legion, the book explores the culture and mindset of the Vlka Fenryka (Space Wolves) leading up to their devastating assault on the planet Prospero and the Thousand Sons Legion. Dan Abnett delivers a character-driven narrative that reframes the Space Wolves as something far more complex than simple barbarians.

## Key Topics
- Space Wolves Legion culture and warrior philosophy
- The events leading to the Burning of Prospero
- The role of remembrancers in the Great Crusade
- The tension between different Legions and their worldviews
- Themes of loyalty, duty, and the nature of the wolf

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to experience one of the pivotal events of the Horus Heresy from the Space Wolves' perspective, told by one of Black Library's finest authors.
