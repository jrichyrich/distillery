# The Ultimate Hang
**Author:** Derek Hansen

## What This Book Is About
The Ultimate Hang is an illustrated guide to hammock camping that covers everything from choosing a hammock and suspension system to staying warm, dry, and bug-free in the backcountry. Hansen provides practical advice on site selection, tarp configurations, insulation options, and gear checklists. The book is designed for both beginners curious about hammock camping and experienced campers looking to refine their setup.

## Key Topics
- Hammock camping basics and terminology
- Suspension systems and anchor points
- Tarp configurations for rain protection
- Insulation strategies for cold weather
- Gear checklists and quick-setup guides

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a practical, illustrated guide to getting started with or improving your hammock camping setup.
