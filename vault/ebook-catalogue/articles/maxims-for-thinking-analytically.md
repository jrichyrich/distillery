# Maxims for Thinking Analytically
**Author:** Dan Levy

## What This Book Is About
Dan Levy distills the wisdom of legendary Harvard professor Richard Zeckhauser into a collection of practical maxims for better analytical thinking. With a foreword by Larry Summers, the book captures decades of Zeckhauser's insights on how to think clearly about decisions, uncertainty, and complex problems.

## Key Topics
- Analytical frameworks for better decision-making
- Thinking under uncertainty
- Heuristics and maxims from a master analyst
- Economic reasoning applied to everyday problems
- Lessons from Harvard's Kennedy School

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want concise, memorable principles for sharper analytical thinking from one of Harvard's most respected minds.
