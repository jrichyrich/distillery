# Discipline Is Destiny
**Author:** Ryan Holiday

## What This Book Is About
Part of Ryan Holiday's Stoic Virtues series, this book argues that self-discipline (temperance) is the foundational virtue that makes all other success possible. Through historical examples ranging from Queen Elizabeth II to Lou Gehrig, Holiday shows how mastering yourself — your body, your temper, your impulses — is the path to freedom and greatness.

## Key Topics
- Temperance as a cardinal Stoic virtue
- Historical examples of disciplined leaders and creators
- Building sustainable habits and self-control
- The relationship between discipline and freedom

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need motivation to recommit to disciplined habits or want historical examples of self-mastery in action.
