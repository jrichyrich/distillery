# Horus Rising
**Author:** Dan Abnett

## What This Book Is About
Book 1 of the Horus Heresy series, set during the Great Crusade as the Emperor's armies conquer the galaxy. Told through the eyes of Captain Garviel Loken of the Luna Wolves Legion, the novel introduces Warmaster Horus at the height of his glory — a charismatic, seemingly incorruptible leader. But the seeds of heresy are already being sown as the Legions encounter forces that will ultimately tear the Imperium apart.

## Key Topics
- The Great Crusade and the Imperium at its zenith
- Horus as Warmaster before his fall
- The Luna Wolves / Sons of Horus Legion
- Philosophy of war, truth, and the Imperial Truth
- The seeds of the galaxy-spanning civil war

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to start the Horus Heresy series from the beginning and experience the Imperium before everything went wrong.
