# Trust and Inspire
**Author:** Stephen M.R. Covey

## What This Book Is About
Trust and Inspire argues that the dominant "command and control" leadership style is outdated and proposes a new paradigm built on trusting people and inspiring greatness. Stephen M.R. Covey, author of The Speed of Trust, shows that in the modern world of work, leaders must extend trust and empower teams rather than manage and control. The book provides a roadmap for becoming the kind of leader who unlocks people's full potential.

## Key Topics
- Why command-and-control leadership is obsolete
- The Trust and Inspire leadership model
- Extending trust to empower teams
- Inspiring performance with purpose
- Overcoming the barriers to trust-based leadership

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to evolve from a controlling management style to one that genuinely trusts and inspires people.
