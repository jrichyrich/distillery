# Ready Player One
**Author:** Ernest Cline

## What This Book Is About
Ready Player One is set in a dystopian 2045 where most of humanity escapes their grim reality by logging into the OASIS, a sprawling virtual universe. When the OASIS creator dies, he leaves behind a series of puzzles rooted in 1980s pop culture, with his entire fortune as the prize. Teenager Wade Watts joins the global hunt, facing corporate villains, impossible challenges, and his own self-doubt in a high-stakes adventure that is equal parts love letter to geek culture and cautionary tale about escapism.

## Key Topics
- Virtual reality and its social implications
- 1980s pop culture, gaming, and nostalgia
- Dystopian society and economic inequality
- The hero's journey in a digital world
- Corporate power versus individual freedom

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a fast-paced, nostalgia-fueled sci-fi adventure that celebrates geek culture while exploring the promise and peril of virtual worlds.
