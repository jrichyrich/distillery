# The Best 376 Colleges
**Author:** The Princeton Review

## What This Book Is About
A comprehensive guide from The Princeton Review ranking and profiling the best 376 colleges in the United States. It provides detailed information about each school including academics, campus life, admissions, and financial aid to help students and families make informed college decisions.

## Key Topics
- College rankings and profiles
- Admissions requirements and strategies
- Campus life and student experiences
- Financial aid and tuition information
- Academic program quality

## When To Reach For This Book
When you or someone you know is researching colleges and needs a comprehensive, data-driven comparison of top U.S. universities.
