# Unlocking Isaiah in the Book of Mormon
**Author:** Victor L. Ludlow

## What This Book Is About
Unlocking Isaiah in the Book of Mormon is a chapter-by-chapter commentary that helps readers understand the Isaiah passages quoted in the Book of Mormon. Ludlow examines Isaiah's symbolic poetry, covenant themes, and messianic prophecies as they appear in 1 and 2 Nephi, making these often-difficult chapters accessible. The book covers Israel's covenant contract, judgment, redemption, and the gathering in the latter days.

## Key Topics
- Isaiah's symbolic and poetic style
- Isaiah chapters as quoted in the Book of Mormon
- Israel's covenant, judgment, and redemption
- Messianic prophecies and their fulfillment
- Latter-day gathering and end-times themes

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a clear, scholarly guide to understanding the Isaiah chapters in the Book of Mormon.
