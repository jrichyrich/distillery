# Divergent
**Author:** Veronica Roth

## What This Book Is About
Set in a dystopian Chicago where society is divided into five factions based on virtues, sixteen-year-old Beatrice Prior discovers she is Divergent — she doesn't fit neatly into any one faction. After making a shocking choice at the Choosing Ceremony, she must navigate brutal initiation, uncover a growing conspiracy, and reconcile who she was raised to be with who she truly is.

## Key Topics
- Identity and belonging in a rigid social system
- Courage, selflessness, and the tension between virtues
- Dystopian government control and conformity
- Coming of age under extreme pressure

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a fast-paced dystopian thriller that explores identity, choice, and what it means to not fit into any single category.
