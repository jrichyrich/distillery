# DIY Lithium Batteries: How to Build Your Own Battery Packs
**Author:** Micah Toll

## What This Book Is About
A practical guide to designing and building custom lithium battery packs from individual cells. The book covers cell selection, pack configuration, battery management systems (BMS), safety considerations, and real-world applications for electric vehicles, solar storage, and portable power systems.

## Key Topics
- Lithium cell types, specifications, and selection
- Battery pack design and configuration
- Battery management systems and safety
- Soldering, spot welding, and assembly techniques
- Applications in EVs, solar, and portable power

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need hands-on guidance for building a custom lithium battery pack for any project.
