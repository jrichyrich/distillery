# Storyworthy
**Author:** Matthew Dicks

## What This Book Is About
Storyworthy is a practical guide to finding, crafting, and telling compelling personal stories. Matthew Dicks, a record-holding Moth StorySLAM champion, shares the techniques he uses to discover storyworthy moments in everyday life and transform them into powerful narratives. The book covers everything from identifying the "five-second moment" of transformation at the heart of every great story to structuring, rehearsing, and performing stories for any audience.

## Key Topics
- Finding storyworthy moments in everyday life
- The five-second moment of transformation
- Story structure: beginning, middle, and the shift
- Techniques for engaging and surprising an audience
- Homework for Life: a daily practice for capturing stories
- Applying storytelling to presentations, writing, and conversation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to become a better storyteller, whether for the stage, the boardroom, the written page, or just around the dinner table.
