# The Cleansing of America
**Author:** W. Cleon Skousen

## What This Book Is About
The Cleansing of America is a sequel to The Majesty of God's Law by W. Cleon Skousen. It examines prophecies and scriptural interpretations related to the future of America from a Latter-day Saint perspective, focusing on themes of national purification, constitutional government, and the prophesied events of the last days. Skousen draws on LDS scripture and the writings of church leaders to build his eschatological framework.

## Key Topics
- Latter-day Saint prophecies about America's future
- Constitutional principles and divine law
- End-times scenarios and preparation
- The role of the United States in prophetic history
- Scriptural foundations for national cleansing

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to explore Latter-day Saint perspectives on American prophecy and end-times theology.
