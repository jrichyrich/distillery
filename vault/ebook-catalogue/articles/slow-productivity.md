# Slow Productivity
**Author:** Cal Newport

## What This Book Is About
Slow Productivity challenges the modern obsession with busyness and pseudo-productivity, proposing instead a sustainable philosophy of meaningful work. Cal Newport, author of Deep Work and Digital Minimalism, argues for doing fewer things, working at a natural pace, and obsessing over quality. Drawing on examples from historical creative thinkers and scientists, the book provides a framework for producing exceptional results without burning out.

## Key Topics
- The problem with pseudo-productivity and busyness culture
- The three principles of slow productivity: do fewer things, work at a natural pace, obsess over quality
- Lessons from historical knowledge workers and creatives
- Strategies for reducing overload and reclaiming focus
- Building a sustainable rhythm of meaningful work

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel overwhelmed by busyness and want a principled approach to doing less but producing work that truly matters.
