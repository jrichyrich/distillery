# How to Fail at Almost Everything and Still Win Big
**Author:** Scott Adams

## What This Book Is About
Dilbert creator Scott Adams shares his unconventional personal success philosophy, arguing that goals are for losers and systems are for winners. Through the story of his many spectacular failures — from restaurant ventures to frozen burrito businesses — Adams distills practical wisdom about building personal energy, developing a talent stack of complementary skills, and designing systems that increase your odds of success even when individual attempts fail.

## Key Topics
- Systems thinking vs. goal-setting
- Building a complementary talent stack
- Personal energy management as the top priority
- The role of failure in eventual success
- Affirmations, diet, fitness, and practical life hacks

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a humorous, contrarian take on personal success that values systems and iteration over rigid goal-setting.
