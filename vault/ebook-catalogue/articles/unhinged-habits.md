# Unhinged Habits
**Author:** Jonathan Goodman

## What This Book Is About
Unhinged Habits is a counterintuitive guide to having more by doing less. Goodman challenges conventional productivity wisdom and argues that reorienting your life around what truly matters -- rather than doing more of everything -- leads to greater fulfillment and results. The book provides a framework for identifying and building the habits that actually move the needle while letting go of the rest.

## Key Topics
- The counterintuitive case for doing less
- Reorienting life around what matters most
- Breaking free from productivity theater
- Building habits that create outsized impact
- Practical strategies for intentional living

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel overwhelmed by doing too much and want a fresh perspective on focusing on what truly matters.
