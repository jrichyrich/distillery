# Influence, New and Expanded
**Author:** Robert B. Cialdini

## What This Book Is About
The definitive work on the psychology of persuasion, updated and expanded with a seventh principle of influence. Cialdini identifies the universal principles that cause people to say yes and explains how to apply them ethically in business and everyday life, while also learning to defend against manipulation.

## Key Topics
- The seven principles of influence: reciprocity, commitment/consistency, social proof, authority, liking, scarcity, and unity
- How compliance professionals exploit psychological shortcuts
- Ethical application of persuasion in sales, marketing, and leadership
- Defending yourself against unwanted influence
- Research-backed behavioral science

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand why people comply with requests and how to be more persuasive or more resistant to manipulation.
