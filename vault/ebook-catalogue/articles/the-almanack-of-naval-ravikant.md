# The Almanack of Naval Ravikant
**Author:** Eric Jorgenson

## What This Book Is About
A curated collection of Naval Ravikant's wisdom on building wealth and finding happiness, compiled from his tweets, podcasts, and essays. The book distills Naval's philosophies on leverage, specific knowledge, judgment, and the pursuit of happiness into an accessible guide covering both the practical mechanics of wealth creation and the deeper pursuit of inner peace.

## Key Topics
- Building wealth through leverage and specific knowledge
- Judgment and decision-making
- The nature of happiness and how to cultivate it
- Mental models for clear thinking
- Philosophy of life, meaning, and fulfillment
- Compounding and long-term thinking

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want concise, high-signal wisdom on wealth creation, happiness, and living a more intentional life.
