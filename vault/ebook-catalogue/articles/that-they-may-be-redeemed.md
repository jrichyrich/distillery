# That They May Be Redeemed
**Author:** Howard W. Hunter and Boyd K. Packer

## What This Book Is About
That They May Be Redeemed is a presentation given at the Regional Representatives Seminar in April 1977 by Elders Howard W. Hunter and Boyd K. Packer. It addresses the scale and urgency of genealogical work and vicarious temple ordinances in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, framing the challenge of redeeming billions of souls through compelling statistics and scriptural context.

## Key Topics
- The scope of vicarious temple work for the dead
- Demographics of baptisms performed vs. total deaths worldwide
- The commandment to perform ordinances for all who have died
- Strategies for accelerating family history work
- Faith in God's plan for universal redemption

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to understand the theological motivation and scale of genealogical and temple work in the Latter-day Saint tradition.
