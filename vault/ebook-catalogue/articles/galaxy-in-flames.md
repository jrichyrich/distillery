# Galaxy in Flames
**Author:** Ben Counter

## What This Book Is About
Book 3 in the Horus Heresy series, this novel depicts the devastating betrayal on Isstvan III where Horus orders the virus-bombing of his own loyal Space Marines. The story follows Loken, Tarvitz, and other loyalists as they realize their Warmaster has turned traitor, leading to one of the most brutal battles in Warhammer 40K lore — brother against brother on a dying world.

## Key Topics
- The Isstvan III atrocity and virus-bombing
- The betrayal of loyal Space Marines by their own Legions
- Brotherhood, loyalty, and the horror of civil war
- The point of no return in the Horus Heresy

## When To Reach For This Book
When you're reading the Horus Heresy series in order and ready for the moment the betrayal becomes irreversible.
