# Malekith
**Author:** Gav Thorpe

## What This Book Is About
The first book in the Sundering trilogy set in the Warhammer Fantasy universe, telling the origin story of Malekith, the Witch King of the Dark Elves. It chronicles his rise from the son of Aenarion, the first Phoenix King, through his betrayal and transformation into one of the most feared villains in the Warhammer world.

## Key Topics
- The origin of Malekith and the Dark Elves
- The Sundering of the Elven kingdoms
- Ambition, betrayal, and corruption
- Warhammer Fantasy lore and world-building

## When To Reach For This Book
When you enjoy Warhammer Fantasy lore and want to understand the origins of the Witch King.
