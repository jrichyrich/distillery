# The CyberSecurity Leadership Handbook for the CISO and the CEO
**Author:** Jean-Christophe Gaillard

## What This Book Is About
JC Gaillard details the security and organizational flaws -- often resulting from decade-old legacy issues -- that leave organizations vulnerable to breaches. The book is aimed at both CISOs and CEOs, showing how to combine organizational and technological resources to defend data in today's threat-rich environment, particularly during digital transformation initiatives.

## Key Topics
- CISO and CEO collaboration on security
- Legacy system security challenges
- Digital transformation security risks
- Organizational security culture and governance
- Data breach prevention strategies
- Combining organizational and technical defenses

## When To Reach For This Book
When you need to align executive leadership and security teams around a unified cyber defense strategy, especially during digital transformation.
