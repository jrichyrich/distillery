# Alpharius: Head of the Hydra
**Author:** Mike Brooks

## What This Book Is About
A Warhammer 40,000 Primarchs novel telling the origin story of Alpharius, the enigmatic primarch of the Alpha Legion. Set in the Horus Heresy era, the book explores the most secretive and deceptive of the Emperor's sons, revealing layers of misdirection, identity, and the Alpha Legion's unique approach to warfare.

## Key Topics
- Origin of Alpharius and the Alpha Legion
- Horus Heresy lore and timeline
- Themes of deception, identity, and loyalty
- Military strategy and unconventional warfare
- Warhammer 40K universe world-building

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to dive into the mystery of the Alpha Legion's primarch or enjoy Warhammer 40K fiction exploring themes of identity and deception.
