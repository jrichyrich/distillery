# Allegiant
**Author:** Veronica Roth

## What This Book Is About
The third and final installment in the Divergent trilogy. Tris and her companions venture beyond the fence that encloses their dystopian Chicago to discover the truth about their society's origins. The story explores themes of identity, genetic manipulation, sacrifice, and the moral complexities of revolution.

## Key Topics
- Dystopian world-building and societal collapse
- Identity and genetic determinism
- Sacrifice and moral courage
- Revolution and its consequences
- Dual-perspective narration (Tris and Tobias)

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to finish the Divergent trilogy or enjoy dystopian YA fiction exploring themes of identity and sacrifice.
