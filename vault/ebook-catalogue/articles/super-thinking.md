# Super Thinking
**Author:** Gabriel Weinberg and Lauren McCann

## What This Book Is About
Super Thinking is a comprehensive guide to mental models -- the big ideas from disciplines like economics, psychology, physics, and philosophy that help you think more clearly and make better decisions. Weinberg and McCann catalog hundreds of mental models and show how to apply them to everyday situations, from career decisions to business strategy. The book serves as a practical reference for upgrading your reasoning toolkit.

## Key Topics
- Foundational mental models across disciplines
- Cognitive biases and how to overcome them
- Decision-making frameworks and heuristics
- Systems thinking and second-order effects
- Probabilistic reasoning and Bayesian thinking

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want a single reference for the most important mental models and frameworks for better thinking.
