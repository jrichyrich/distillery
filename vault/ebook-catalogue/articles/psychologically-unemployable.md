# Psychologically Unemployable
**Author:** Jeffery Combs

## What This Book Is About
Psychologically Unemployable is written for people who realize their talents are worth more than any traditional job could pay them. Jeffery Combs argues that many of the world's most successful entrepreneurs share a common trait: they are "psychologically unemployable" -- driven by dysfunction, dissatisfaction with conventional work, and a deep need to build something of their own. The book provides a framework for channeling that restless energy into entrepreneurial success.

## Key Topics
- The entrepreneurial mindset and why some people cannot thrive in traditional jobs
- Turning personal dysfunction into a catalyst for success
- Building a business on your own terms
- Financial freedom through entrepreneurship
- Mentorship and personal development for entrepreneurs

## When To Reach For This Book
When you feel trapped in traditional employment and want motivation and practical insight for building your own entrepreneurial path.
