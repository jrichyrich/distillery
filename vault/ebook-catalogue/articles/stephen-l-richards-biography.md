# Stephen L Richards: A Biography
**Author:** W. Dee Halverson

## What This Book Is About
This biography chronicles the life of Stephen L Richards (1879-1959), a prominent leader in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who served in the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles and later in the First Presidency. W. Dee Halverson traces Richards' journey from his country boyhood and family heritage through his career as an attorney, his calling to the apostleship, and his lasting legacy of service and leadership.

## Key Topics
- The Richards and Stayner family heritage
- Stephen L Richards' early life and education
- His career in law before full-time church service
- Calling to the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles
- Service in the First Presidency
- His lasting legacy in church and community

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want to learn about the life and contributions of Stephen L Richards, a significant but less widely known figure in Latter-day Saint church history.
