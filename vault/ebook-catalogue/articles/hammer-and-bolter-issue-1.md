# Hammer and Bolter: Issue 1
**Author:** Black Library (Various Authors)

## What This Book Is About
The first issue of Black Library's digital fiction magazine featuring short stories and serialized fiction set in the Warhammer 40,000 and Warhammer Fantasy universes. Each issue collects multiple tales from various authors, offering bite-sized adventures across the grim darkness of the far future and the Old World.

## Key Topics
- Short fiction from the Warhammer universes
- Multiple authors and diverse storylines
- Warhammer 40K and Warhammer Fantasy settings
- Serialized and standalone stories

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want quick Warhammer fiction across multiple settings and authors without committing to a full novel.
