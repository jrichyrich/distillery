# Void Stalker
**Author:** Aaron Dembski-Bowden

## What This Book Is About
Void Stalker is the third and final novel in the Night Lords trilogy set in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. It follows Talos and First Claw as the warband faces its reckoning -- hunted by enemies and torn apart by internal conflict. Dembski-Bowden delivers a powerful conclusion to one of the most acclaimed series in Warhammer fiction, exploring loyalty, damnation, and what it means to be a villain protagonist.

## Key Topics
- The conclusion of the Night Lords trilogy
- Talos and First Claw's final battles
- Themes of loyalty and damnation among traitors
- Warhammer 40,000 Chaos Space Marine lore
- Villain protagonists and moral complexity

## When To Reach For This Book
When you have read the first two Night Lords novels and want to see how Talos's story ends.
