# Darth Plagueis
**Author:** James Luceno

## What This Book Is About
A Star Wars novel exploring the life of Darth Plagueis the Wise, the Sith Lord who trained Palpatine (Darth Sidious). The story spans decades, chronicling Plagueis's obsession with conquering death through manipulation of the Force, while his apprentice secretly plots to surpass him and seize control of the galaxy.

## Key Topics
- The master-apprentice relationship between Plagueis and Sidious
- Sith philosophy and the Rule of Two
- Manipulation of galactic politics leading to the prequel era
- The dark side of the Force and midichlorian experimentation

## When To Reach For This Book
When you want deep Star Wars lore about the Sith and the political machinations behind the fall of the Republic.
