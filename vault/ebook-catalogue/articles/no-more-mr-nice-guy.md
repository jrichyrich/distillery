# No More Mr. Nice Guy
**Author:** Robert A. Glover

## What This Book Is About
Therapist Robert Glover identifies the "Nice Guy Syndrome" -- a pattern of seeking approval by trying to please everyone while suppressing one's own needs -- and provides a proven plan for breaking free. The book helps men reclaim personal power, set boundaries, build authentic relationships, and pursue a more fulfilling life in love, sex, and career.

## Key Topics
- The Nice Guy Syndrome and its origins
- Learning to prioritize your own needs
- Reclaiming personal power and masculinity
- Building honest, authentic relationships
- Strategies for a more satisfying love life and career

## When To Reach For This Book
When you recognize patterns of people-pleasing and approval-seeking that are holding you back in life and relationships.
